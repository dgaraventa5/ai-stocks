# AAPL — Context Briefing (2026-06-17)

Prepared as a `/refresh-context` pass for an off-watchlist scoring exercise. Apple is the largest
company NOT in the watchlist; it is being scored to quantify *why* it is / isn't an AI-supply-chain
pick, not to force inclusion. AI directness is expected to be modest — the goal is to apply the
rubric honestly. Satisfies the rule-12 research gate.

Latest reported quarter: **fiscal Q2 2026 (quarter ended 2026-03-28, reported 2026-04-30 after close)**.
Apple's FY runs Oct–Sep, so "Q2 FY26" = the March 2026 quarter.

---

## 1. Pre-research mental model (verbatim, written before pulling data)

> "Apple is a consumer-electronics + services franchise, not an AI-infrastructure supplier. Its AI
> angle is on-device/edge AI ('Apple Intelligence') running on its in-house A/M-series silicon, plus
> a 'Private Cloud Compute' tier on Apple-silicon servers. Apple Intelligence has been a laggard /
> embarrassment — the personalized-Siri overhaul was promised for 2025, slipped badly, and Apple has
> reportedly been forced to lean on a third-party frontier model (Google Gemini?) to power the
> revamped Siri. Apple is the single largest consumer of TSMC leading-edge wafers, so it is upstream
> demand for the foundry layer but is itself a buyer/consumer, not a seller of AI compute. Services
> is the growth/margin engine (~45%+ of gross profit) and faces a regulatory overhang (DOJ antitrust,
> EU DMA, and the Google search-payment dependency that the Google monopoly remedy threatens). China
> is the key geographic risk — both demand (Greater China revenue had been declining) and supply
> (most assembly still in China, tariff-exposed). I do NOT expect Apple to score as a direct AI-supply
> -chain name; its AI Thesis directness should be low. The interesting questions are (a) does Apple
> build its own AI server silicon, making it a genuine upstream TSMC AI-demand driver, and (b) is the
> valuation (~30x fwd P/E) pricing in an AI re-rate it hasn't earned."

3. **Apple–Google Gemini deal (announced 2026-01-12).** Apple pays Google ~**$1B/yr** for a custom

   features targeted iOS 26.4 (spring 2026); full Siri-as-chatbot redesign anticipated iOS 27 (WWDC

   reportedly sourcing glass-substrate samples directly — deeper vertical integration. **This is the
   one genuinely "AI-infra-buyer" development**: if real, Apple becomes a material new buyer of TSMC
   leading-edge AI-inference silicon + Broadcom design services in 2027+.
   https://appleinsider.com/articles/26/01/13/ming-chi-kuo-apple-will-get-serious-about-ai-server-chips-in-2026 ;

5. **TSMC leading-edge position — Apple is lead N2 (2nm) customer.** TSMC entered N2 high-volume
   manufacturing Jan-2026; Apple is "traditionally TSMC's lead customer" and is using N2 for the A20 /
   A20 Pro (iPhone 18, spring 2027) and M5-series chips. Apple's annual A/M-series volume is the single
   largest block of TSMC leading-edge wafers — Apple is the demand anchor that funds each node, ahead
   of NVDA/AMD/Google AI silicon.
   https://www.techspot.com/news/110755-tsmc-2nm-n2-process-officially-enters-volume-production.html ;

   EU DMA exposure continues. Separately, the **Google search-monopoly remedy** threatens Apple's
   ~$20B/yr Google default-search payment (the largest single high-margin Services line) — an indirect
   but very large Services-revenue tail risk.
   https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/United_States_v._Apple_(2024) ;

## 5. DIFF since training / prior mental model

1. **China demand has re-accelerated hard** — Greater China +28% YoY (Q2 FY26), reversing the decline
   I carried. China is now a tailwind on the demand leg, even as the asset/supply leg (assembly
   concentration, tariffs) remains a structural risk.
2. **iPhone is NOT ex-growth right now** — +22% YoY March-quarter record. My "ex-growth hardware,
   services is the only engine" model is half-wrong this quarter (though 3y rev CAGR is still only 1.8%
   — the TTM strength is recent).
3. **Apple is renting the frontier (Gemini ~$1B/yr)** — concrete confirmation Apple's own models lag;
   Private Cloud Compute hosts a third-party model. AI is a *cost/feature*, not a revenue line.
4. **Baltra in-house AI server silicon is real and near** (H2-2026 MP, 2027 datacenters, Broadcom
   partner). This is the only thread that makes Apple a genuine *upstream AI-infra buyer* — but it's
   2027+ and inference-for-own-services, not merchant AI compute.
5. **Apple is the #1 TSMC N2 customer** — confirms its role as the foundry-layer demand anchor (upstream
   pull-through), though as buyer not seller.
6. **Valuation at top of its own 3-yr range** (P/S 91st pctile; fwd P/E ~31x) on a ~1.8% 3y revenue
   CAGR — expectations flag fired; the multiple embeds an AI upgrade cycle not yet earned.

## 6. Implications for ratings (flag only — not pre-deciding)

- **Layer assignment is genuinely ambiguous** (see return summary). Apple is consumer hardware +
  services; it does not map cleanly to any supply-chain layer. Best fit is **Layer 10 (Models,
  Software & Applications)** as an edge-AI *platform/distribution* owner (1.4B-device install base is
  the AI distribution channel), but it is an imperfect fit and the map has no consumer-device /
  edge-AI-platform home — flag a map gap. (Note: if Layer 10, rule 10 says the Value column's
  EV/EBITDA cell should hold EV/FCF — but unlike the high-SBC SaaS cohort rule 10 targets, Apple has
  large clean positive EBITDA, so both metrics are meaningful; see recommendation.)
- **AI Thesis D1 (AI revenue %):** ~0 as a reported line item — Apple has *no* disclosed AI revenue;
  Apple Intelligence is a free feature, and it *pays* $1B/yr for Gemini. Low.
- **AI Thesis D5 ("is Apple itself an AI-infra buyer"):** today minimal (asset-light, ~2.4% capex/rev,
  rents Gemini); the Baltra/2027-datacenter plan is forward optionality, not committed disclosed capex.
- **Risk R2 (geo):** the live debate — China demand leg improved (+28%) but the asset/tariff leg and
  the policy leg remain; ~18% of revenue is Greater China. Frame the single worst shock explicitly.
- **Risk R4 (regulatory):** material, multi-front — DOJ monopoly suit, Epic injunction already biting
  Services margin, EU DMA, and the Google-payment tail. This is a genuine 2-territory rating.
- **Momentum:** beat-and-raise quarter + Greater China reversal support M1; but 50DMA % only 50.8%
  (price action choppy, not a clean uptrend) and the AI-laggard narrative caps relative strength.
