## 6. Implications for ratings (FLAGS ONLY — do not pre-decide; R5 defaults 5 for non-Layer-10)

**Rule 13 — EV/MW Value object (the critical one):** Value here is secured gross MW per capacity-mw.json, NOT income-statement metrics (revenue is mining-only and falling by design; GAAP losses are large). capacity-mw.json currently: **907 MW, basis "207 mining + ~700 HPC under construction," as-of 2026-05-05, High confidence.** Two issues to resolve before trusting the denominator at the next rescore:
  - (a) The json basis line lists "3rd campus 100, Stingray 100" as *separate* 100 MW items; web/business-update language is **207 mining + 600 contracted HPC ≈ 807 MW Operating & Contracted Gross.** The 907 may be ~100 MW high (possible double-count of Stingray vs. an unnamed third campus, or inclusion of a not-yet-contracted site). **Flag for reconciliation** — a stale/overstated MW denominator understates EV/MW (flatters Value), the rule-13 hazard.
  - (b) Disclosure basis is mixed gross-vs-IT: Barber Lake is "168 MW critical IT → 300 gross"; AWS/Black Pearl "~300 gross." Confirm whether the 600 HPC figure is gross (no x1.25 conversion needed) before recomputing. The as-of date (2026-05-05) is ~7 weeks old but no new MW disclosed since; **not clearly stale, but the basis needs the §6(a) reconciliation.**

**AI Thesis (D1–D5) — where bitcoin→AI conversion belongs (rule 13, not Value):** This is now a genuinely contracted AI pivot, not speculative: AWS + Fluidstack/Google as named anchors, $11.4B backlog, 600 MW contracted HPC, GPU-ready (H100/Blackwell). Material positive evidence for D1/D2 directness and D5 contract durability vs. the untouched-template thesis. Counter-flag: HPC revenue **has not started flowing** — first commencements Sept–Q4 2026; execution/construction risk remains. Do not over-credit backlog as realized.

**Momentum (M / 50DMA):** Stock rose on the Q1 print; refresh `scripts/momentum_50dma.py` at any rating pass (objective input, not set here).

**Risk:**
- **R1 customer concentration:** Now highly concentrated in essentially **two anchor leases** (AWS 300 MW, Fluidstack/Google 300 MW) — but both are top-tier / hyperscaler-backed credit (Google guarantees $1.4B of Fluidstack). Concentration HIGH but counterparty quality HIGH — net a judgment call to flag, not resolve.
- **R3 balance sheet:** **~$5.2B total debt** (mostly nonrecourse, project-tied) vs $715M cash, $714M equity, $1.118B accumulated deficit, ongoing operating losses, and ATM share-sale reliance. Leverage + financing/dilution stress is a real flag — but nonrecourse, asset-backed, contracted-cash-flow-matched structure mitigates. Flag the tension; don't pre-score.
- **R5 Disruption Risk:** Layer 09 (physical power/datacenter infra) → **defaults to 5** per rule 16. No change.

**Rule 14 (expectations red-flag):** `expectations_flag.py` likely self-skips or misfires here — GAAP-loss, mining-revenue-falling-by-design name; P/S vs revenue-growth screen is not meaningful while the business model is mid-transition. Note as not-applicable rather than forcing it.

**THESIS-BREAK watch (none triggered this pass):** monitor for (1) AI-deal slippage/cancellation or commencement delays past Sept 2026 (Barber Lake) / Q4 2026 (Black Pearl Phase I, Stingray); (2) financing/dilution stress given $5.2B debt + continued ATM use; (3) bitcoin-price exposure as mining winds down (declining but still the only current revenue); (4) construction delays. No break observed — all contracts intact, financing closing on schedule.
