My prior: FY2026 data-center revenue somewhere around $800M-1B run-rate, Climate Solutions approaching or exceeding half of total revenue with low-to-mid-20s segment EBITDA margins, and the stock's re-rating riding on DC mix shift."

4. **PT spin-off via Reverse Morris Trust with Gentherm (announced 2026-01-29, close expected Q4 CY2026):** post-spin MOD is a pure-play climate/DC company. My model had "pruning/divesting," not a full exit.
