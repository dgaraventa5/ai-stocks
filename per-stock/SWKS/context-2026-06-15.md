## Business mix & customer concentration (RED FLAG)

- **Mobile = 58% of Q2 FY26 revenue; Broad Markets = 42%** (transcript).
- **Largest customer ≈ 60% of total revenue** (transcript Q2 FY26) — this is **Apple**. This is a severe single-customer concentration risk and the central bear case: Apple has been dual-sourcing / in-sourcing RF content, and SWKS content-per-iPhone has been pressured. SWKS does not name Apple in the release but the ~60% figure aligns with prior Apple disclosure (10-K risk factors historically cite one customer >60%).
- **Broad Markets growth engines:** Wi-Fi, data center, and automotive collectively **+30% YoY**; data center expected to grow **~50% annually** off a small base (transcript). Broad Markets overall +10% YoY.
- **New design win:** "Multi-generational Android OEM design win with expected $1B+ revenue through 2030" (Q2 FY26 release headline) — a diversification positive away from Apple, but spread over ~5 years.

## AI / datacenter exposure & directness (assess honestly — LOW)

SWKS is an **RF front-end and analog/connectivity** supplier; it is **not a core AI-compute or AI-infrastructure name.** Genuine AI-supply-chain content:
- "Data center" is called out as a Broad Markets growth vector (timing/clocking, signal-conditioning, power-isolation, and connectivity content into servers) growing ~50%/yr, but **off a small base** — data center is a minority slice within the 42% Broad Markets segment (i.e., low-single-digit % of total revenue, not separately quantified in the release).
- **Estimated genuinely AI-supply-chain revenue: low single digits % of total (~3–5%, not separately disclosed — FLAG as estimate).** The overwhelming majority of revenue is smartphone RF (Apple/Android) and non-AI broad markets (automotive, IoT/Wi-Fi, infrastructure).
- **AI Thesis directness: LOW.** SWKS is a handset RF company with a small, growing data-center connectivity attach. It does not gate, accelerate, or uniquely enable AI compute. Any AI exposure is indirect and immaterial to the consolidated P&L today.

## Major pending event — QORVO MERGER (transformative, must flag)

- **2025-10-27:** Skyworks agreed to **acquire Qorvo (QRVO)** in a stock-and-cash deal: Qorvo holders receive **0.960 SWKS shares + $32.50 cash per share**; pro forma ownership ~**63% SWKS / 37% Qorvo**. (8-K, accession 000110465926064574; PRE/DEF 14A FY2026.)
- **Regulatory:** FTC issued an **HSR Second Request** (waiting period extended) — deal is NOT closed and carries antitrust risk (two of the three largest RF front-end suppliers combining). Mutual termination fee $298.7M; SWKS pays $100M to Qorvo under certain regulatory-failure scenarios.
- Implication: standalone SWKS financials are about to be materially restated by a large M&A combination if it closes. This is the single biggest swing factor on the name — far more than AI exposure. Score the standalone entity but the thesis is dominated by deal completion/synergy/antitrust risk.
