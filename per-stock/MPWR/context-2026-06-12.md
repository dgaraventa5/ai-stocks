### Finding 1 (NEW — not in 2026-05-25 briefing): Financial restatement + material weakness in internal controls
MPWR disclosed (8-K **Feb 26, 2026**, reiterated in an Apr 30 filing) that **certain previously

four quarters of FY2025**. Root cause: a **material weakness in internal control over financial

margin, non-GAAP opex, non-GAAP net income/EPS, or the Q1 2026 outlook**, and that **no management
or employee misconduct** was involved. Q1 2026 earnings call made **no mention** of it.

## 5b. EXPECTATIONS VERDICT — what is priced in, and what would break it

**What is priced in (the bull case the 97th-pctile multiple requires to be true):**
- Enterprise Data compounds off the +98% YoY base, with the 85% growth floor holding into H2 2026;
- MPWR **wins and ramps the Vera Rubin power socket at high share** (the projected ~$420M/2027 +
  >$4 EPS), i.e. the Blackwell loss was a one-platform stumble, not structural;
- MPWR captures meaningful 800V HVDC content for 2027 Rubin Ultra/Kyber racks;
- Margins hold ~55%+ and the model keeps delivering operating leverage.
At ~27x sales / ~110x earnings, the multiple is **pricing the recapture as if confirmed.**

**What would break it (the falsifiable bear triggers):**
- **Vera Rubin socket disappoints** — MPWR's actual VR200 share comes in materially below the
  projected ~70% (Infineon/Renesas repeat the Blackwell displacement). This is the single biggest
  break risk because it is *the* thing the multiple assumes and the company has *not* confirmed.
- **Enterprise Data growth floor gets *cut*** (the inverse of the serial raises) — would signal the
  ordering/backlog visibility that justified the multiple was channel-fill, not end-demand. Watch the
  Q2 print (early Aug 2026) and the inventory trend.
- **Restatement/material-weakness escalates** — remediation slips, scope widens beyond deferred taxes,
  or it draws SEC/governance attention. Currently contained (non-cash, tax-only) but unresolved.
- **General AI-capex digestion** — MPWR is now a high-beta proxy on NVIDIA platform spend; a
  hyperscaler capex pause hits a 27x-sales name far harder than a 5x name.

**Verdict:** The 97th-percentile multiple is riding the AI-power narrative **plus an unconfirmed
re-acceleration story**, not a confirmed one. The Q2 *guide* genuinely re-accelerates (≈35–36% YoY)
and Enterprise Data momentum is real and filing-verified — so this is **not** a hollow "great business,
priced for perfection, decelerating" case like a saturated name. But the keystone assumption (Vera
Rubin high-share recapture) is **sell-side projection the company won't confirm**, layered on a name
that *already demonstrably lost the prior platform* to the same competitor now "leading" the next
standard. The expectations flag is correctly identifying that the price embeds a recapture that is
not yet in evidence. This is a real asymmetry to weigh in Value/Risk — not a thesis-break, but a
"don't let momentum stand in for forward return" caution exactly as rule 14 intends.
