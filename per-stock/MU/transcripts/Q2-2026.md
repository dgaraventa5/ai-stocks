## Management Tone

CEO Sanjay Mehrotra was highly confident on AI demand durability. Key themes:
- Demand exceeds supply across all AI memory products
- HBM4 development on track; entered preliminary customer sampling
- 1γ DRAM and G9 NAND nodes ramping — fastest DRAM node ramp in company history
- New fabs under construction: Idaho, New York, Taiwan, Singapore
- First multi-year long-term agreement (LTA) signed with a large AI customer
- FY2026 capex raised to $25B (from prior $20B guidance)
- Dividend raised 30% to $0.15/quarter
