## 1. Pre-research mental model

ONTO is a Layer 04 semi-equipment name (metrology, inspection, lithography software) whose differentiated AI exposure is back-end **advanced packaging / HBM inspection** — the Dragonfly optical-inspection franchise sits at the yield-control chokepoint for 2.5D logic and HBM stacking, exactly where AI-compute build-out is most capacity-constrained. Calendar fiscal year. Going in, I expected a high-growth, high-margin (~55% non-GAAP GM) story with advanced-packaging revenue compounding well above WFE, the main risks being cyclicality (back-end orders are lumpier than front-end WFE), customer concentration in a handful of HBM/foundry leaders, and China/export exposure. The open questions were whether order momentum was still accelerating into 2026 and whether margins were holding.

## 2. Latest filing highlights

Primary source: **8-K (filed 2026-05-05, Item 2.02), Exhibit 99.1 earnings release** for Q1 FY2026 (quarter ended March 31, 2026). All figures below from that release unless noted. 10-Q filed 2026-05-05 (period 2026-03-31).

**Top line / margins (Q1 2026 vs Q1 2025):**
- Revenue **$291.9M, +9.5% YoY** (vs $266.6M Q1 2025); a record, +~10% sequential vs Q4 2025 (8-K Ex99.1, 2026-05-05).
- GAAP gross margin **50.1%** vs **53.7%** Q1 2025 — **~360bp YoY compression** (8-K Ex99.1). COGS $145.6M vs $123.4M, i.e. COGS +18% on revenue +9.5% (income statement, 8-K Ex99.1).
- Non-GAAP gross margin **55.7%** vs 55.1% — *up* 60bp YoY (8-K Ex99.1). **Flag:** the GAAP/non-GAAP GM gap widened to ~560bp, vs ~140bp a year ago.
- GAAP operating income **$33.5M (11.5% of rev)** vs $63.1M (23.7%) Q1 2025 — **GAAP operating margin halved** (8-K Ex99.1).
- Non-GAAP operating income **$77.9M (26.7%)** vs $76.5M (28.7%) — non-GAAP OM down ~200bp YoY (8-K Ex99.1).
- GAAP diluted EPS **$0.67** vs $1.30 Q1 2025; Non-GAAP diluted EPS **$1.42** vs $1.51 (8-K Ex99.1).

**GAAP→non-GAAP bridge driver (rule-1 sourcing):** the release defines non-GAAP exclusions as "amortization of intangibles, merger and acquisition-related expenses and benefits, litigation expenses and benefits and other restructuring costs" (8-K Ex99.1). The GAAP margin erosion is tied to **Semilab USA acquisition** integration (intangibles amortization + likely inventory step-up flowing through COGS) and M&A costs — i.e. largely non-cash / one-time-flavored, not core-business margin decay. **Per rule 15 this is the kind of non-operating-dominated distortion to watch, but here it hits margin/op-income, not a clean EPS-YoY one-time gain.**

**Cash / balance sheet:**
- Operating cash flow **~$26M** in Q1 2026 (8-K Ex99.1) — notably *low* vs $33.8M GAAP net income / $70.8M non-GAAP net income (working-capital build during the ramp; flag for FCF-conversion check per rule 11 companion).
- Ended Q1 with **$654M cash + short-term investments** (8-K Ex99.1).
- Capital structure event (prior news-log, 8-K 2026-05-18/05-21): priced **$1.3B 0.00% convertible senior notes due 2031**; $205.0M used to repurchase 805,325 shares @ $254.53 concurrent with pricing; capped calls; remainder funds the **$710M / 27% Rigaku stake** and general corporate purposes.

**Q2 2026 guidance (8-K Ex99.1):**
- Revenue **$320M–$330M** (≈ +10–13% sequential, a meaningful acceleration off Q1).
- Gross margin **56.0%–56.5%** (non-GAAP); GAAP operating margin **17.8%–18.7%**; non-GAAP operating margin **28%–28.6%**.
- GAAP diluted EPS **$1.09–$1.18**; non-GAAP diluted EPS **$1.65–$1.73**.

**Segment color (8-K Ex99.1 + transcript, see §3/§4):** Advanced nodes +13% in the quarter, guided to ~25% full year; Atlas G6 OCD selected by a second logic customer for gate-all-around metrology; Dragonfly G5 qualified at a leading 2.5D logic customer and an HBM customer.

## 6. Implications for ratings (FLAGS ONLY — not pre-deciding)

- **D1 (AI directness / demand pull):** Revisit upward-bias candidate. AP is the differentiated AI driver and is the *fastest-growing* segment (>50% FY guide), with an explicit HBM4 design win and a multi-year HBM volume agreement. Among the most direct back-end AI-inspection beneficiaries. *Confirm the >$240M figure and the AP mix % before finalizing.*
- **D5 (durability of AI driver / moat):** Revisit. HBM4 qualification + 3Di + sub-200nm resolution + diversification across >10 customers strengthens the durability/breadth of the packaging franchise; Rigaku (X-ray) and Semilab (surface-charge) broaden the process-control moat. Counter-consideration: Camtek competes directly in AP inspection — assess competitive intensity before lifting.
- **Growth:** Inputs now stale-bullish if last refreshed pre-Q1. Revenue YoY +9.5% (Q1) understates the run-rate; Q2 guide and FY >30% imply acceleration. **Rule-9 objective refresh is warranted** (Q1 reported, beat >0% but not >15%; margin did NOT move >500bp on a non-GAAP basis, so this is "within 1 week," not "immediate"). **TTM caveat (rule 9):** TTM will lag the accelerating run-rate.
- **Quality / margins:** Watch the **GAAP vs non-GAAP divergence**. GAAP GM/OM fell hard; non-GAAP held/improved. If the scoring inputs pull GAAP operating margin, they'll mis-score quality downward on acquisition amortization. Flag for the human: decide GAAP vs non-GAAP treatment consistent with house convention. **FCF conversion red-flag (rule 11 companion):** Q1 OCF ~$26M vs net income $33.8M GAAP / $70.8M non-GAAP — low conversion this quarter (working-capital build in the ramp); qualitative flag, not a scored metric.
- **Momentum (M / 50DMA%):** Beat-and-raise + record backlog + positive aftermarket reaction are supportive; refresh `scripts/momentum_50dma.py` for the objective 50DMA% input (rule 11).
- **R1 (customer concentration):** Still HBM/foundry-leader heavy, but G5 across >10 customers is a mild de-concentration. No new disclosed single-customer % — flag the data gap.
- **R3 (balance sheet):** New $1.3B 0% convert (due 2031) + $710M Rigaku outlay changes the balance-sheet picture; offset by $654M cash and 0% coupon. Re-examine the R3 input — leverage up, but cheap and cash-cushioned.
- **R5 (disruption risk):** **Defaults to 5** per rule 16 (Layer 04 physical semi-equipment, not Layer 10). No change.
- **Expectations red-flag (rule 14):** Run `python3 scripts/expectations_flag.py ONTO` before rating — stock near highs (~$300) into peak AI-packaging enthusiasm; check whether P/S sits ≥90th percentile of its 3-yr range against growth. Qualitative counterweight only.

**THESIS-BREAK watch (checked, status):** Order decel — NONE (record backlog, accelerating Q2 guide). Customer-concentration adverse shift — NONE (diversifying). Guidance cut — NONE (raised). Margin compression — **PARTIAL FLAG:** GAAP margins compressed materially, but driven by Semilab/M&A amortization with non-GAAP margins holding/expanding; not a core-economics break. **No thesis break.**
