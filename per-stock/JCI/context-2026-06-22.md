## 1. Pre-research mental model

JCI is a diversified commercial-buildings company (applied HVAC/chillers, building controls, fire & security, OpenBlue software) that has reshaped itself into a near-pure-play commercial-buildings systems provider after selling its Residential & Light Commercial HVAC business to Bosch (2025). The AI angle is data-center thermal management — chillers, applied cooling, liquid cooling — a fast-growing but minority pocket of total revenue, riding AI-datacenter buildout. Activist (Elliott) involvement and a leadership change have been catalysts for margin/portfolio simplification. Expect strong orders/backlog with data-center pull, but the core remains broad commercial buildings, not a pure AI name — keep AI-directness (D1) honest.

## 6. Implications for ratings (FLAGS ONLY — not pre-deciding)

- **D1 (AI directness):** Keep honest. DC thermal is the growth *driver* and the marketing centerpiece, but data center is a MINORITY share of JCI's total commercial-buildings revenue (exact % not disclosed in this pass — gap). JCI's revenue base is broad commercial buildings (HVAC, controls, fire & security, service), not AI infrastructure. Do not let the strong DC narrative inflate D1 beyond the actual revenue mix.
- **D2 (customers/demand):** Orders +30%, record $20B backlog (+26%), Americas-led DC strength, guidance raise — positive signal; revisit alongside the rule-9 objective refresh.
- **D5 / moat:** Differentiated applied chillers (YDAM, YK-HT), liquid-cooling M&A (Alloy), OpenBlue software, large installed base + service backlog ($2.5B recurring-ish) support durability. R5 (Disruption Risk) — JCI is NOT Layer 10; **R5 defaults to 5** (physical infra/HVAC, no agentic-AI disruption to core model).
- **Momentum (M):** Strong fundamental momentum (orders, backlog, EPS, guidance raise). 50DMA% (rule 11) is a separate objective refresh — not computed here.
- **Risk (R1–R4):** Portfolio-reshaping (Bosch divestiture, ~$5.0B net proceeds) is a balance-sheet/capital-allocation item to weigh in R3; Ireland domicile (R2 geography) is structural, not new. Activist (Elliott) + leadership change are governance context — note but not adverse.
- **Rule-14 (expectations / what's-priced-in):** JCI has rallied on the DC-cooling theme and just raised guidance — momentum/value mix may sit where consensus is crowded. Multiple flags are already elevated in this briefing (guidance-prior discrepancy, 84% vs 40% order-growth conflict, 8-K/A amendment, unverified primary-exhibit numbers). Run `scripts/expectations_flag.py JCI` as part of any scoring pass; treat current optimism as a qualitative caution, NOT a scored input.
- **THESIS-BREAK watch:** None triggered. Backlog accelerating (not decel), demand strengthening, guidance raised (not cut), margins expanding (adj EPS +45% on +6% organic). Monitor for: backlog/order deceleration in Q3 FY26, any DC-orders air-pocket, guidance walk-back.
