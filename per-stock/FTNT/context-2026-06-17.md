# FTNT (Fortinet, Inc.) — Context Briefing 2026-06-17

Legal name: **Fortinet, Inc.** | Proposed layer: **10 — Models, Software & Applications (AI cybersecurity)** | Peer set: CRWD, PANW

## 1. Pre-research mental model

What I believed about FTNT before this pass:
- Fortinet is the firewall/secure-networking leader, built on its proprietary ASIC (FortiASIC / SPU) hardware-accelerated FortiGate appliances and FortiOS — a hardware-heavy, appliance-anchored security vendor, contrasted with cloud-native CRWD.
- Revenue ~$6-7B, growing low-to-mid teens; profitable with strong (~80%) gross margins and high FCF generation; consistently net cash.
- Three-pronged story: legacy firewall/secure networking (the cash cow), unified SASE (the growth pivot), and security operations / SecOps (AI-driven). A multi-year firewall refresh cycle (the 2020-era FortiGate installed base coming due) was the near-term product-revenue catalyst.
- AI angle: FortiAI (genAI assistant across the Security Fabric) plus the structural tailwind that AI expands the attack surface to defend. But Fortinet is an AI *user/beneficiary*, not AI infrastructure — its AI thesis is indirect, similar to CRWD but more hardware-tethered.
- Founder-led (Ken Xie, CEO); heavy ongoing insider selling under 10b5-1 plans is the chronic optics issue.
- Channel-only go-to-market (two-tier distribution) → distributor concentration risk.
