## CEO Seamus Grady — Prepared Remarks

### Optical Transceiver Demand (800G / 1.6T)

- DCI growth "fueled by strong growth in a wide range of products," primarily 800G ZR modules
- Datacom constrained by component shortages: "Demand during the quarter far exceeded what we were able to ship" — constraints across "lasers, memory...and certain ASICs"
- Two new 800G scale-out transceiver programs qualified and shipping to a hyperscale customer; initial ramp in Q4, "volumes to ramp steadily throughout fiscal 2027"
- Multiple merchant transceiver programs qualified for scale-out applications; production expected H2 calendar 2026 (early FY2027)
- 1.6T transceiver production entered production in H2 FY2026; steep ramp expected in FY2027

### AI Networking / Hyperscaler Customer Demand

- HPC segment hit $107M (+25% QoQ), supporting customer technology transition
- $150M quarterly HPC milestone pushed out one quarter (now ~Q2 FY2027) due to technology transition, but "overall trajectory is stronger" with additional follow-on products expanding opportunity beyond $150M
- Grady: "Growth is accelerating...we're probably more optimistic than we've been in quite some time"

### Co-Packaged Optics (CPO)

- Minority investment in Raytech Semiconductor: ~$32M for ~14% stake
- Working with 3 customers on CPO programs — shipping to all three
- Both scale-up and scale-out applications
- Revenue "largely in front of us at this point" — still early stage

### Manufacturing Capacity / Thailand Expansion

**Building 10** (2M sq ft addition):
- First floor operational June 2026
- Additional floor by September-October 2026
- Full completion January 2027
- Adds ~$3B revenue capacity

**New Navanakorn facility:**
- $11M acquisition (8-acre campus)
- Current 200K sq ft building under renovation
- Provides ~$250M initial capacity
- Future expansion potential on remaining acreage

**Total capacity roadmap:**
- Current: ~$4.8B
- With all expansions (Pinehurst, Building 10, Navanakorn): ~$8.5B
- Future Buildings 11-12 potential: additional ~$3B

Capital allocation on capacity: management noted ROI is "relatively straightforward" — 6 months of operating profit at full capacity covers entire 2M sq ft building cost.

### Margin Commentary

- Gross margin 12.1%, down 30 bps QoQ from FX headwinds and new program ramp inefficiencies
- Expect "similar to Q3" at low-12% range near-term
- Grady: "as these programs mature, we do expect those efficiencies to improve and get back to our higher margin ranges"
- Operating leverage improving: OpEx at 1.4% of revenue (improving YoY)

### Supply Chain Constraints

- Constraints characterized as temporary: "Component supply ecosystem is doing everything they can to catch up...there is a lag right now"
- Broadening constraints in datacom: lasers, memory, ASICs
- Does NOT reflect demand weakness — industry growth exceeding supply capacity
- Near-term supply volatility expected, long-term improvement
