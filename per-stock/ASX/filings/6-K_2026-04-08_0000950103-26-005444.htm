ASE TECHNOLOGY HOLDING CO., LTD.
Files 2025 Annual Report On Form 20-F

 

Taipei, Taiwan, R.O.C., April 8, 2026 – ASE Technology Holding Co., Ltd. (NYSE: ASX, TAIEX: 3711, “ASEH” or the “Company”), announces that it has filed its annual report on Form 20-F for the year ended December 31, 2025, with the U.S. Securities and Exchange Commission.  The 2025 20-F is available on ASEH's website at www.aseglobal.com and on the website of the U.S. Securities and Exchange Commission at www.sec.gov.  Hard copies of the audited financial statements included in the 2025 Form 20-F are available to shareholders upon request and free of charge.  To request a copy of the audited financial statements, please contact Citibank Shareholder Services at 1-877-CITI-ADR (248-4237).
