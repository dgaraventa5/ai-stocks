## 1. Pre-research mental model (verbatim, written before any searches)

> BESI is the leading supplier of hybrid bonding (direct copper-to-copper, no bumps) die-attach equipment, the critical tool for sub-10-micron-pitch advanced packaging used in HBM stacking and logic-on-logic 3D integration. I believe their key customers are TSMC, Intel, ASE, AMD, and memory makers, with TSMC being the anchor for hybrid bonding ramp. As of my training my last clear read was roughly Q3/Q4 2025; orders were recovering off a cyclical trough, with hybrid bonding being a small-but-fast-growing slice (most revenue still from mainstream die-attach/packaging for autos/mobile/photonics). Financials reported in EUR; high gross margins (~60%+), strong FCF, net cash, Dutch domicile. Roadmap centered on HBM4 and chiplet adoption driving a 2025-2027 hybrid bonding inflection.

## 5. DIFF: what changed since my training

- **Cyclical trough is OVER — now in a sharp up-cycle.** My model said "orders recovering off a trough." Reality: Q1-26 orders +104.5% YoY, hybrid-bonding unit orders at all-time high, Q2-26 guided +30-40% rev QoQ. This is an inflection, not a recovery.
- **Hybrid bonding is now a near-majority growth driver, not a "small slice."** ~€36M (2023) → ~€476M (2026E), ~1/3 of revenue. Much bigger/faster than my mental model.
- **China is the #1 geography at 46% of Q1-26 revenue** — higher than I assumed. Combined with Asia-Pac (41%), 87% of revenue is Asia. This is a geo/export-risk and customer-concentration consideration I had under-weighted.
- **AMAT is an explicit D2W hybrid-bonding partner** for HVM — collaboration not just competition. ASMPT is the main competitor.
- **Insider: Chairman bought ~€9.7M open-market in last 12 months** — I had no insider data; this is a strong M3 signal.
- **Valuation has re-rated hard:** trailing PE ~133, forward ~54. Stock near 52-wk high (€291 high vs €103 low — roughly a 2.8x range in a year). Momentum is extreme.
- **HBM4-on-microbumps nuance:** memory hybrid-bonding transition may slip; logic/foundry is the surer near-term driver. Mild negative vs the pure HBM4 bull case.
