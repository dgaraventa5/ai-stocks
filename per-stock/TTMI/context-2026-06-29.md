## 5. Implications for ratings (describe the signal — no numbers assigned)

- **D1 (AI exposure / directness):** Confirmed and arguably firmer — ~80% of sales now AI + defense, DC&N 36% and growing, explicit high-layer-count/ultra-HDI/SLP + liquid-cooling content tied to AI accelerators. Signal: stable-to-supportive of the existing AI-Thesis rating; nothing here weakens directness.
- **D2 (demand durability / backlog):** Book-to-bill ~1.35-1.41, $1.6B A&D backlog, formalized Investor Day targets and a doubling-EPS-by-2027 goal. Signal: supportive — order momentum and multi-year visibility intact.
- **D5 (moat / differentiation):** The European acquisitions (STG, ILFA) add advanced microcircuit / rigid-flex / A&D / medical capability and a domestic-to-EU reshoring angle; the thesis rests on ultra-high-layer-count work Asian low-cost competitors can't easily replicate. Signal: mildly supportive of differentiation, but M&A integration is unproven — watch execution.
- **Momentum (M):** Price +169% YTD, +27% trailing 30 days, "Strong Buy" consensus with rising targets, beat-and-raise + Investor Day catalysts. Signal: strongly positive; 50DMA% almost certainly elevated. (Objective 50DMA% refresh via `momentum_50dma.py` if rescoring.)
- **R1 (customer concentration):** ~80% AI+defense concentration and hyperscaler-driven DC demand — concentration is a watch item, not newly worse. Neutral-to-watch.
- **R2 (geography):** **Increasing** geographic complexity — existing China data-center expansion PLUS new Switzerland/Germany operations and a cross-border M&A integration (regulatory approvals pending). Signal: R2 risk modestly higher / more to monitor.
- **R3 (balance sheet):** Mixed. Refi lowers borrowing cost ~50bps and terms out to 2030 (positive), but capex raised to $300-320M, FY26 FCF only "slightly positive," ~$912M debt, and **two all-cash acquisitions** will consume cash/add leverage (price undisclosed). New restricted-payment covenants. Signal: balance-sheet risk roughly stable-to-slightly-elevated until deal size is known — **flag the undisclosed purchase price.**
- **R4 (regulatory):** Two pending regulatory approvals for the EU deals; defense work adds the usual oversight. Minor incremental.
- **R5 (disruption):** Non-Layer-10 hardware/defense infra — defaults to most-durable per rule 16; no AI-disruption signal to the core PCB/interconnect model. No change.
- **Value:** Unchanged tension — ~85.8x P/E, third-party DCF (~$130) below price (~$200+). Stretched on absolute bands regardless of growth. The capex raise pressures FCF yield further. Signal: Value remains the weak leg.
