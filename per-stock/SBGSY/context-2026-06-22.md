## 1. Pre-research mental model

Schneider Electric is a French electrical-equipment and energy-management major (low/medium-voltage distribution, switchgear, UPS via APC, EcoStruxure software, plus Industrial Automation). It is a leading AI-datacenter beneficiary on the power-and-cooling side — datacenter power architecture, UPS, busway, and liquid cooling (Motivair) — sitting upstream of the racks themselves. It reports in EUR on a half-year (H1/FY full results) cadence with Q1/Q3 revenue-only trading updates, so there is no US-style quarterly 10-Q; the US listing is an ADR. Going in I expect strong datacenter-driven organic growth and a double-digit FY profit target, with the main risk being valuation (consensus crowded) and FX, not demand.

## 2. Latest results highlights

**Source: Schneider Electric Q1 2026 Revenues release / presentation, published ~Apr 2026 (se.com).** Reported as a Q1 revenue-only trading update (not full P&L — Schneider only gives full results at H1/FY). **All figures are in EUR — see foreign-ADR currency trap note below.**

- **Group revenue: €9.77bn in Q1 2026, +11.2% organic** — described as record revenues; beat expectations (release-q1-revenues-2026, se.com; corroborated investing.com Q1 2026 earnings-call transcript, Apr 2026).
- **Energy Management +12.8% organic; Industrial Automation +4.4% organic** (se.com Q1 2026 presentation). Datacenter is the lead driver inside Energy Management.
- **By business model:** Systems (33% of rev) +16% organic — strongest; Products (48% of rev) +9%; Software & Services (19%) +9% (se.com Q1 2026 presentation, Apr 2026).
- **By region:** North America +14.4% organic; China & East Asia +14.2% organic (se.com Q1 2026 presentation).
- **FX headwind:** −€623m / −6.7% of Group revenue in Q1 2026; FY 2026 FX impact estimated −€750m to −€850m at current rates (se.com Q1 2026 release).
- **FY 2025 context (prior full-year, se.com FY2025 release):** record revenue ~€40bn; **backlog €25.4bn at YE2025, +18% YoY** — backlog growth outran the ~8.9% organic sales growth, with datacenter-linked backlog skewed to 2027-and-beyond execution. This is the cleanest forward-visibility signal.
