## 1. Pre-research mental model

LITE is a direct AI-datacenter optical play: it makes the EML and CW lasers, photonic chips, and transceivers (800G/1.6T) that hyperscalers consume inside AI clusters, riding the high-speed optics supercycle, plus a declining legacy industrial/consumer-laser tail. Expectation going in: strong cloud/AI-driven revenue growth and margin recovery, but lumpy GAAP profitability from acquisition/one-time items (rule-15 watch) and meaningful customer concentration. Open questions: pace of the EML/transceiver capacity ramp, whether hyperscaler demand is decelerating, and what's priced in after a large run.

## 6. Implications for ratings (FLAGS ONLY — not decisions; collaborate per rule 12)

- **D1 (AI exposure / directness):** Pure-play AI-optics; >60% revenue AI+cloud, undershipping demand >30%, NVIDIA $2B + purchase commitment. Among the most direct Layer-07 names — candidate to confirm/raise if currently understated. Research-backed now (this briefing satisfies the rule-12 gate; thesis.md is still the untouched template — flag for population).
- **D2 (capacity/ramp signal):** Two consecutive +40% EML capacity expansions, fifth InP fab (Greensboro), NVIDIA-funded U.S. fab. Strong, but new-fab output ~2028 — near-term volume is capacity-constrained (could cap upside even with demand).
- **D5 (moat / durability):** ~50–60% global EML share; EML is the industry's 1.6T bottleneck; NVIDIA co-development + capacity-access rights deepen lock-in. Supports a high moat/durability read.
- **Momentum (M):** Record revenue, beat, ninth straight quarter of laser-assembly growth, +700bps op-margin QoQ — strong fundamental momentum. Refresh objective **50DMA %** via `scripts/momentum_50dma.py` before any momentum re-rate (rule 11) — not run this pass.
- **R1 (customer concentration):** Single-name risk is real (Google a top customer; NVIDIA now both investor and multi-bn buyer). NVIDIA LTA/purchase commitment partially structurally de-risks, but concentration is the key R-flag to weigh. **THESIS-BREAK watch:** any future hyperscaler order deceleration or NVIDIA/Google demand pullback.
- **R3 (balance sheet):** Improved — $2.02B cash inflow, $650.4M converts swapped to equity (only $172.2M converts left). Net: stronger, modest dilution (~0.8M incremental shares on principal + 5.0M delivered).
- **R5 (disruption risk):** Layer 07 physical optical infra — **defaults to 5** per rule 16 (no agentic-AI disruption to the optical-component revenue model; AI is a tailwind). No change expected.
- **Rule 14 (expectations / what's priced in):** Run `python3 scripts/expectations_flag.py LITE` before scoring — stock has run hard on the AI-optics narrative + NVIDIA deal; check for peak-P/S-with-decelerating-growth flag (growth is clearly *not* decelerating here, so the flag may not trigger, but document it). Qualitative only.
- **Rule 15 (EPS YoY):** **Do not blank.** GAAP EPS swing is operationally driven; only small restructuring + a $27.5M nine-month escrow benefit are non-operating. Net out the escrow benefit when reading GAAP but keep EPS YoY as a scored input.
