## 1. Pre-research mental model

SMCI is the Layer 08 (Servers) AI rack-scale leader — GB200/GB300 GPU servers and direct-liquid-cooling (DLC) racks for hyperscalers/neoclouds, a direct NVIDIA Blackwell ramp beneficiary, but structurally a low-gross-margin box assembler (single-digit GM, competition-compressed) sitting on a still-unresolved governance/accounting overhang (2024 E&Y resignation, delayed filings, Nasdaq delisting scare since cured, DOJ/SEC subpoenas). The investable question is whether a thin-margin, high-revenue-velocity business can fund an explosive working-capital ramp without diluting shareholders or relapsing on filings. Key watches: gross margin trajectory, governance/filing integrity, customer concentration, and liquidity/cash burn.

## 6. Implications for ratings (FLAGS ONLY — do not pre-decide; collaborative rating session owns the numbers)

**Quality — REVISIT (down-pressure).** Confirmed structurally thin gross margin (GAAP 9.94% Q3, 6.4% Q2, Q4 guided 8.2–8.4%) and now-negative operating cash flow (−$7.56B 9M). This is a low-margin, cash-consuming box assembler — Quality inputs (GM, FCF margin, ROIC) should be refreshed off the 10-Q, and note the TTM-vs-MRQ volatility (rule 9 TTM limitation cuts BOTH ways here — margins are volatile, not monotonically improving, so neither TTM nor MRQ is clearly "right"). Flag the negative FCF-conversion red-flag (rule 11 companion check).

**Risk R1 (customer concentration) — REVISIT (down-pressure).** Top-1 32.2%, top-4 ~75% (10-Q Q3 FY2026). High by any standard.

**Risk R3 (balance sheet / liquidity) — REVISIT (down-pressure).** Cash $1.29B, 9M operating cash flow −$7.56B, net debt →$7.5B, debt >$8.7B, ~$6.3B emergency raise at a 7.00% mandatory-convert coupon (vs GOOGL 6.25%). Liquidity is the live risk.

**Risk R4 (regulatory/governance) — REVISIT (down-pressure).** DOJ + SEC subpoenas remain OPEN with no formal resolution; new ~May-2026 securities class action; related-party purchases ($202.8M/qtr, growing). The governance overhang is active, not historical — do not let "delisting resolved / special committee cleared" understate this.

**Growth — REVISIT (mixed).** +122.7% revenue YoY is genuine and operational (rule 15: this is a real operational number off a real base, not one-time-dominated — keep it). But -19% QoQ and the FY guide range ($38.9–40.4B) temper momentum. EPS YoY is operational here, not one-time-driven — no rule-15 blanking.

**AI Thesis D1–D5 — REVISIT (likely supportive, but check directness).** Direct, large NVIDIA GB300/B300 + AMD MI355X DLC exposure, ~70% DLC share claim, ~$39B backlog — strong AI directness (D1/D2). BUT moat (D-moat) is weak: commodity assembly, intense competition (Dell/HPE/ODMs) is exactly what compresses the margin. Don't let backlog size inflate the moat dimension.

**Momentum (incl. 50DMA %) — REVISIT.** Stock +20% post-earnings then -31% on the raise — high volatility. Refresh 50DMA % via `scripts/momentum_50dma.py`; subjective EPS-revisions rating should reflect the EPS beat ($0.84 vs $0.62) but RS is whipsawed.

**Rule 14 (expectations red-flag) — RUN `python3 scripts/expectations_flag.py SMCI`.** SMCI is a hyper-cyclical, heavily-debated name; check whether P/S is at a high percentile of its own range against decelerating sequential growth. Qualitative red flag for the briefing only.

**R5 (Disruption Risk) — defaults 5.** Layer 08 (Servers), not Layer 10 — no agentic-AI disruption to the core revenue model. R5 = 5 per rule 16 (the governance/margin/liquidity risks live in R1/R3/R4, not R5).

**THESIS-BREAK WATCH (none triggered, but two amber):**
- (a) **Liquidity/cash burn** — −$7.56B 9M operating cash flow + $6.3B raise: a clear amber. A further raise or covenant stress would escalate.
- (b) **Governance relapse** — filing was on time and special committee cleared, but DOJ/SEC subpoenas remain open and a new class action was filed. A subpoena escalation, a late filing, or an adverse DOJ/SEC finding would be a thesis-break.
- (c) Margin guided down to 8.2–8.4% but not collapsed; backlog intact; no customer loss disclosed. No outright break this pass.
