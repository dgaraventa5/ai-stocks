## 1. Pre-research mental model

ASML is the Layer-04 (Semi Equipment) keystone: a near-absolute monopoly in EUV lithography (and the dominant DUV vendor), making it the single gating tool for every advanced-logic and advanced-DRAM/HBM node. The bull case is that the AI capex super-cycle pulls forward both logic (TSMC sub-2nm) and memory (HBM4/DRAM 1c) lithography intensity, so ASML compounds regardless of which chip vendor wins. The two live risks are (a) China — historically ~30%+ of sales, now being squeezed by US/Dutch export controls, and (b) the High-NA EUV ramp converting from a science project (Intel) into a broad revenue line on schedule. Coming in I'd expect a strong order book but China revenue stepping down hard in 2026.

## 3. WebSearch findings

1. **Q1 2026 system-sales mix — EUV-heavy.** Net system sales €6.3B, of which **EUV €4.1B** (~65% of system sales); GM 53.0% at high end of guidance, driven by EUV mix. Total net sales ~+13% YoY vs ~€7.8B Q1-2025; down sequentially from €9.7B Q4-2025. — StockTitan 6-K summary / search recap, 2026-04-15. https://www.stocktitan.net/sec-filings/ASML/6-k-asml-holding-nv-current-report-foreign-issuer-728274d6f07a.html

2. **Memory > logic for the first time ever.** In Q1 2026 memory end-use was **51% of system sales vs logic 49%** — first quarter memory has exceeded logic, driven by HBM (HBM3E/HBM4/HBM4E) and DRAM 1c-node EUV intensity. — Digitimes 2026-04-21. https://www.digitimes.com/news/a20260421PD211/asml-demand-revenue-hbm-equipment.html

3. **China stepping down hard.** China was ~33% of total revenue in 2025 (ASML's largest single market); ASML guides China to **~20% of revenue in 2026**, and **China was only ~19% of net system sales in Q1 2026 vs ~36% in Q4 2025** — export-control normalization already biting. — Search recap of CNBC/Fool, Apr 2026. https://www.fool.com/investing/2026/04/20/asml-just-got-hit-by-a-new-china-export-threat-is/

4. **NEW China export threat — "MATCH Act."** Shares fell >4% on 2026-04-07 after US lawmakers proposed legislation to restrict sales/servicing of **DUV** tools to China; could cut China share below the 20% assumption. The 20% FY26 guide reflects *existing* restrictions only, not this proposal. — Global Banking & Finance / Yahoo, Apr 2026. https://www.globalbankingandfinance.com/asml-shares-fall-us-congress-plan-restrict-china-exports/

5. **High-NA EUV (EXE:5200) moving to early HVM.** First EXE:5200 shipped (175 wph, +60% productivity vs predecessor); Intel accepted two EXE:5200 units at Oregon for 14A (HVM-accepted, targeting 2027); Samsung received first EXE:5200B (2nd unit H1 2026) for 1.4nm; imec qualifying for Q4 2026 sub-2nm. Still early — most customers in test/qual, not volume. — TrendForce 2025-07-17 / Bits&Chips / Tom's Hardware. https://www.trendforce.com/news/2025/07/17/news-asml-confirms-first-high-na-euv-exe5200-shipment-reportedly-prepping-for-intels-14a-in-2027/

6. **Record EUV order from SK hynix.** SK hynix disclosed it will buy **~$7.9–8B of ASML EUV equipment through end-2027** (up to ~30 EUV machines) for HBM/advanced DRAM — largest single publicly disclosed EUV order ever. DRAM makers adding ~250k wafers/month; 1c node lifts litho intensity to ~28%. — Tom's Hardware / wccftech, 2026. https://www.tomshardware.com/tech-industry/semiconductors/sk-hynix-places-record-8-billion-order-for-asml-euv-lithography-machines

7. **2027 capacity signal.** Fouquet said ASML could deliver **~80 low-NA EUV machines in 2027 "if customer demand really underpins" it** — a strong forward-capacity statement, not a guarantee. — search recap of earnings coverage, 2026-04-15. https://finance.yahoo.com/markets/stocks/articles/asml-holding-nv-asml-q1-070054037.html

## 6. Implications for ratings (FLAGS ONLY — no numbers pre-decided)

- **EUR FOREIGN-ADR CURRENCY TRAP (must flag at scoring):** ASML reports in EUR; yfinance USD price ÷ EUR financials makes **P/S, EV/EBITDA, FCF-yield** unreliable (USD÷EUR garbage). Per `project_foreign_adr_currency_trap` memory: **blank** those three Value inputs and lean on **forward P/E + PEG** (yfinance converts forward EPS correctly), flagged **low-confidence**. A blind rule-9 re-pull will re-corrupt these — re-blank after any refresh. Same-unit ratios (gross margin, FCF *margin*, ND/EBITDA, rev CAGR/YoY, EPS YoY) survive.
- **D1 (AI thesis directness/demand):** flag for review — demand commentary unambiguously stronger (order intake "very strong," FY26 raised, memory>logic crossover, SK hynix record order). Likely supports a high D-dimension but DO NOT set a number here.
- **D2 (customer/end-market breadth):** flag — memory now co-equal with logic; broadens the demand base beyond TSMC-logic.
- **D5 / moat & secular durability:** flag — EUV monopoly intact; High-NA broadening reinforces it.
- **Momentum (M, rule 11):** flag — refresh 50DMA% via `scripts/momentum_50dma.py`; note the ~4% drop on the MATCH-Act headline (2026-04-07) and sequential sales dip — momentum reading needs the objective metric, not narrative.
- **R1 (customer concentration):** unchanged-to-slightly-better — memory/logic balance reduces single-segment reliance.
- **R2 (geography) — flag, deteriorated:** China step-down (33%→~20%) plus NEW DUV-restriction proposal (MATCH Act) is a genuine geographic/regulatory risk that may warrant a harsher R2; this is the live debatable dimension.
- **R3 (balance sheet):** strong — €8.4B cash+ST after €1.1B buyback and dividend; FCF generative.
- **R5 (Disruption Risk, rule 16):** **defaults to 5** — ASML is Layer-04 physical infra/semi equipment, not Layer-10; no sub-5 R5.
- **Growth (rule 15 check):** EPS YoY likely operationally driven (no obvious one-time-dominated swing flagged) — keep unless a filing documents a non-operating-dominated change.
- **rule 14 expectations flag:** `scripts/expectations_flag.py` self-skips foreign filers (no us-gaap XBRL) — note the gap; assess "what's priced in" qualitatively (consensus is crowded-long, and SALP carries a put — see news-log 2026-06-12).

**THESIS-BREAK watch:** none triggered. Bookings strong (not collapsing), guidance RAISED (not cut), High-NA on track (not delayed). The one watch item that could escalate to a thesis dent is the **MATCH Act / DUV-to-China restriction** if enacted beyond the embedded 20% assumption — monitor, not yet a break.
