# ZS (Zscaler) -- Context Briefing 2026-06-17

Legal name: **Zscaler, Inc.** | Proposed layer: **Layer 10 (Models, Software & Applications -- AI cybersecurity)**, peer of CRWD/PANW/FTNT. Fiscal year ends July 31.

## 1. Pre-research mental model

What I believed about ZS before this pass:
- Zscaler is the zero-trust cloud security leader (Zero Trust Exchange / SSE / SASE), pioneered cloud-delivered secure web gateway and ZTNA, displacing legacy VPN/firewall appliances.
- Revenue ~$2.5-3B, growing mid-20s% YoY, very high gross margins (~78-80%), GAAP near-breakeven but strongly FCF-positive.
- AI thesis: dual -- uses AI for threat detection/data classification (Avalor acquisition 2024) AND secures AI (data loss prevention on AI app usage, securing AI workloads).
- Founder-CEO Jay Chaudhry, large insider ownership.
- Premium SaaS multiple; stock had run up strongly into 2025.
- Competes with CRWD, PANW, Netskope, Cisco, Microsoft.

### Key surprises
1. **The growth thesis took a real hit** -- 16-17% FY27 guide is a structural step-down, not a one-quarter blip; partly execution (sales attrition), partly maturity.
2. **The valuation reset is the other side of that coin** -- this is now a cheap-ish SaaS name (EV/FCF ~21x, P/S at a 3-yr low), not a crowded momentum trade. Value and Momentum will pull in opposite directions.
3. **AI product motion is genuine** -- AI Protect and the agentic-AI/MCP security suite are early but monetizing, and structurally well-aligned to the AI buildout (more AI agents = more zero-trust surface to broker).

R4 ordinary-course litigation only -> high. **But note: rubric Risk dimensions don't capture the execution/growth-deceleration risk that just materialized -- that lives in the thesis narrative, not R1-R4.**
