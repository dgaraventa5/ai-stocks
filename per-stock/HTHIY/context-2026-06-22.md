## 6. Implications for ratings (FLAGS ONLY — no numbers pre-decided)

- **D1 (direct AI exposure / criticality):** Strengthened. Grid/transformer bottleneck is a hard physical constraint on AI-datacenter buildout; 40-month lead times + $43B backlog + AI-specific products (X LABS energy parks, NVIDIA 800 VDC) argue for a closer look at whether D1 is currently understated. Caveat: Hitachi Energy is one segment of a large conglomerate — AI revenue is diluted at the consolidated HTHIY level; weigh segment exposure vs. group dilution.
- **D2 (moat / supply position):** Strengthened — entrenched HVDC/transformer position, multi-year backlog, capacity expansion others can't quickly match.
- **D5 (terminal-value / business-model durability):** Positive — physical grid infra with a 10–15+ yr demand runway; X LABS/PaaS move extends the value chain. R5 for HTHIY = **default 5 (non-Layer-10 physical infra).**
- **M (momentum):** Re-examine. Record results, raised shareholder-return program, and AI-grid newsflow are positive; the lone "EPS miss" headline (April print, unverified) is a possible offset. Refresh the objective **50DMA %** via `scripts/momentum_50dma.py` before finalizing — note HTHIY ADR liquidity/price-history quality may affect the metric.
