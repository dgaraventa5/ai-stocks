## 1. Pre-research mental model

CEVA is a sub-$1.1B pure-play wireless + edge-AI IP licensor whose thesis rides on AI/NPU licensing (>20% of licensing revenue) converting into a royalty inflection in 2027+ as customers tape out and ramp (Toyota RAV4 via Renesas R-Car V4H is the first mass-volume AI royalty stream). The economics are GAAP-unprofitable with ~20%-of-revenue SBC, modest single-digit revenue growth (8–12% guided FY26), heavy customer concentration (top 3 ≈ 48% of Q1 2026 revenue), and minimal FCF — offset by ~$216M net cash and a Bluetooth/Wi-Fi/UWB connectivity moat. As of the May 27 briefing the stock traded ~$38 on a licensing-growth + AI-optionality narrative at ~73x forward non-GAAP P/E. My prior expectation entering this refresh: nothing structurally has changed in ~1 month, and the next true data point (Q2 2026 earnings) is not due until ~early August, so this is a between-quarters maintenance pass.
