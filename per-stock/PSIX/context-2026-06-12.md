### 2b. FCF-conversion red-flag check
FY2025 OCF $24.1M ÷ net income $114.0M = **21% conversion** — well below 60%. BUT this is the heavy-capex/working-capital-build signature the rule explicitly says NOT to flag as accrual games: receivables + inventory grew to support the data-center order ramp (net sales +52%, OCF −61% is the working-capital tell of a fast-growing hardware manufacturer, not earnings-quality manipulation). **Conditional flag does NOT fire** on the accrual-games reading; HOWEVER it is a genuine cash-quality watch item — profits are not yet turning into cash, and a class action alleges the demand/cost story was overstated. Flag as **cash-conversion watch, not accrual-fraud signal.**
