## 1. Pre-research mental model

Knowles is an industrial-tech pure-play (post the Dec-2024 Consumer MEMS Microphone divestiture to Syntiant for $150M) with two segments — Precision Devices (capacitors + RF, ~56% of Q1 rev) and MedTech & Specialty Audio (~44%). The thesis is defense/medtech/electrification, not AI: Q1 2026 beat (rev $153.1M +15.8%, non-GAAP EPS $0.27 +50%) was driven by defense replenishment and the Cornell-Dubilier specialty-film ramp ($75M+ energy order), with no disclosed AI/data-center revenue. The AI angle is product-capability-only (capacitors for server PSU power delivery, supercaps for GPU buffering) with no customer pull, so D1 directness should remain thin. Stock has run hard (+130% 1yr as of mid-May, +55% YTD), so much of the transformation is priced in.
