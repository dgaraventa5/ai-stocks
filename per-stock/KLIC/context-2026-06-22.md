## 1. Pre-research mental model

Going in: KLIC is a cyclical back-end semi-equipment name whose core wire-bonder franchise is commodity-ish and was cyclically weak; the bull case rests on advanced-packaging optionality (TCB/hybrid bonding for HBM and chiplets) actually ramping into real revenue. Expectation was a still-soft core with a small, early advanced-packaging story — i.e., a "show me" AI angle layered on a depressed cyclical base. thesis.md is the untouched template, so there is no prior populated per-name research to anchor against; the news-log (2026-05-05) flagged a Q2 beat and a sharp guidance step-up, suggesting the cyclical trough is behind it. KLIC also sits on the R2-rubric review list (project memory).
