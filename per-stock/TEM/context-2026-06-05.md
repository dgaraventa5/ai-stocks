## 6. Implications for ratings (flag only)

- **D1 AI Revenue %:** Whole company is AI-native precision medicine; Data & Applications (~25%) is explicitly AI/foundation-model revenue, and Diagnostics is algorithm-augmented (~40% attach). Likely 4-5.
- **D2 Position / D3 Moat:** 500PB proprietary linked clinical-molecular dataset + Ambry/Deep 6 + regulatory (FDA-cleared assays) = real data moat, but Spruce Point disputes durability and diagnostics is competitive (Guardant, Foundation/Roche, Natera in MRD). Moat debatable 3-4.
- **D4 Capacity:** Active expansion, new verticals (Alzheimer's), MRD ramp, FDA approvals — but capex-light software/lab model. Moderate.
- **D5 Hyperscaler Exposure:** N/A in the classic sense; customers are pharma, not hyperscalers. Low (1-2) on supplier framework.
- **M1 EPS Revisions:** Revenue guide raised but GAAP EPS missed badly; mixed. Check Yahoo/Zacks revision trend before scoring.
- **M2 Relative Strength:** Stock recovered to ~$53 post-FDA from mid-$40s; needs 3-6mo vs benchmark check (IGV or biotech/XBI).
- **M3 Insider:** Continuous Lefkofsky selling, but all 10b5-1 pre-arranged → per rubric §M3 step 2, treat as routine → default ~3 unless % of holdings is extreme. No insider buying surfaced.
- **R1 Customer Concentration:** ~half a dozen pharma at $100M+ TCV; data segment lumpy/concentrated, but diagnostics revenue is highly diversified (payors/oncologists). Net moderate — 3-4; flag data-segment lumpiness.
- **R2 Geo Risk:** US-centric, no China/export-control exposure → likely 5.
- **R3 Balance Sheet:** ~$640M cash, no acute leverage, but burning FCF (-$83M/qtr) and not yet FCF+; convert/credit facility + covenants. Moderate 3-4.
- **R4 Regulatory/Litigation:** Spruce Point short report + dual-class governance + Lefkofsky promoter history + FDA-regulated diagnostics + de-identified health-data privacy exposure. Elevated → 2-3.

**Thesis-break watch:** (1) Spruce Point accounting allegations validated (revenue/EBITDA restatement) would be existential — monitor for SEC inquiry/auditor change. (2) Data-segment TCV is bookings, not recognized revenue — watch renewal/conversion. (3) FCF must inflect positive on the ~$65M EBITDA path or financing risk returns.

**Limited-history flag:** IPO June 2024 → no clean 3-year revenue CAGR available (only ~2 yrs public). FCF is **negative** (-$83M Q1), so the Layer-10 EV/FCF metric is **not computable as a positive multiple** — score it as worst-band (negative FCF) and rely on EV/S + growth instead; note in Watchlist.
