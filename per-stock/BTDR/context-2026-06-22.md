## 1. Pre-research mental model

Bitdeer is a vertically-integrated bitcoin miner that designs its own SEALMINER ASICs (SEAL-series chips), runs a large self-mining fleet, and is pivoting power-rich sites (Clarington OH, Tydal Norway, Bhutan, US) toward HPC/AI colocation. It is structurally different from pure miners because the ASIC-design business is real optionality (chips it can sell or self-deploy) and Tether is a ~20% strategic shareholder. The capacity-mw.json carries 2,479 MW secured (1,744 online + ~735 committed) as of 2026-04-30. Key uncertainties going in: how much of the GW capacity is energized AI-grade vs BTC-mining-only; whether the AI/HPC pivot is converting to contracted revenue or is still pipeline; and financing/dilution stress given heavy capex and a recent convert raise.
