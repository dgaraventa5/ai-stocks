## 1. Pre-research mental model

LRCX is the etch + deposition duopolist (with AMAT/TEL) in Layer 04 semi equipment — most levered to memory (NAND/DRAM) WFE, with growing foundry/logic exposure via gate-all-around (GAA) and a structurally rising advanced-packaging/HBM tailwind. Coming off a memory-spend downcycle into recovery; the bull case is AI-driven HBM4 + GAA + NAND tech transitions lifting served market per wafer. The dominant risk has long been outsized China revenue mix (~30%+) and US export-control exposure that could be cut by policy at any time. Going in, I expected a strong but cyclically-sensitive quarter with China as the swing factor.
