## 1. Pre-research mental model

WULF is a low-cost, zero-carbon-powered (nuclear-adjacent / NYPA hydro) bitcoin miner at Lake Mariner, NY, pivoting hard into HPC/AI hosting. Going in, the mental model was: ~438 MW of contracted critical IT (Fluidstack ~360 + Core42 + Abernathy 168), Google backstopping Fluidstack's lease obligations for an ~8% equity stake, with the thesis hinging on actually energizing and converting that contracted backlog to recurring lease cash flow. The Value object per rule 13 is secured gross MW, not income statement — GAAP is deeply loss-making during the build. Open questions: how much is energized vs. contracted, dilution/financing stress from the build-out, and whether any AI leases have slipped.

Flag: **the MW denominator needs a clean rebuild from the CB-building roster (CB-2/3/4/5 + Core42 + Abernathy) before trusting EV/MW.** Muskie (1 GW, 2028/2030) stays EXCLUDED.
