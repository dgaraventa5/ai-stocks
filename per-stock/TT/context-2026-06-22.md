## 1. Pre-research mental model

TT is the HVAC/climate-control leader (Trane commercial + Thermo King transport refrigeration), a broad commercial/industrial franchise with strong pricing power, high ROIC, and a long-cycle services tail. The AI angle is a fast-growing but still **minority** pocket: data-center thermal management (chillers, applied systems, and now liquid cooling via the LiquidStack and Stellar Energy acquisitions plus an NVIDIA AI-factory partnership). The core thesis is durability/quality with a data-center *kicker* — not a pure-play AI name, so D1 directness must stay honest against the large non-AI HVAC base. Prior data point (news-log 2026-05-12): record bookings, all-time-high $10.7B backlog, NVIDIA partnership, LiquidStack closed March 2026.
