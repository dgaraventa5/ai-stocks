## 1. Pre-research mental model

IREN is a renewable-powered Bitcoin miner pivoting hard into AI Cloud (GPU-as-a-service), with the rule-13 thesis being that its real asset is secured gross MW of grid-connected power/land, not its income statement (GAAP losses during the pivot). Prior mental model: ~2.9 GW secured (capacity-mw.json, as-of 2026-05-07, "High" confidence), a $3.4B NVIDIA AI-cloud contract and a recently-closed $3B convertible (1.00%, due 2033) driving the AI-cloud build-out. Expected staleness risks: rapid MW pipeline growth, AI-cloud revenue still tiny vs. mining, and heavy dilution/financing as the GPU capex ramps. Treated as Australian-incorporated 20-F/6-K filer — needs verification (it actually now files 10-Q/8-K).

## 6. Implications for ratings (FLAGS ONLY — do not pre-decide)

- **Rule-13 EV/MW Value object (CRITICAL):** capacity-mw.json currently holds **IREN = 2,910 MW** (basis "company secured power and land portfolio, gross transmission MW," as-of 2026-05-07, "High" confidence). The Q3 update discloses **4,510 MW of executed grid-connection agreements across 7 named sites** — materially above 2,910. **Possible-stale flag:** either (a) the json's 2.9 GW reflects "grid-connected & contracted" (energized + firm) while 4,510 MW includes longer-dated executed-but-not-energized capacity (Sweetwater 2, Oklahoma 1.6 GW, etc.), or (b) the json simply pre-dates the disclosure. Per the json's own definition (energized + committed/under-construction; speculative pipeline excluded), the 1.6 GW Oklahoma and Sweetwater 2 600 MW need a construction-status check before inclusion; the 800 MW Australia (2028) and broader 5.8 GW pipeline are clearly speculative and **excluded**. **Recommend a deliberate capacity-mw.json re-basis decision before any EV/MW rescore** — a wrong denominator moves Value directly (rule 13). Do NOT fold AI-conversion probability into MW (that's AI-Thesis's job per rule 13).
- **AI Thesis (D1–D5):** AI-cloud conversion is materially de-risked vs prior — **$9.7B Microsoft + $3.4B NVIDIA contracts, $3.1B ARR under contract (~84% of target booked), NVIDIA taking a ~$2.1B equity stake.** This is a strong directness/customer-quality signal (hyperscaler + the GPU vendor itself). Revisit D1 (AI revenue directness), D2 (customer quality), D5 (catalysts). AI-cloud revenue +839% YoY supports an upgrade case — but flag: still only ~23% of total revenue, contracts phase in through 2026.
- **Momentum (M / rule 11):** Stock ran hard (news-log: $72 in May; "92% surge" coverage). 50DMA% to be refreshed via `scripts/momentum_50dma.py` at any scoring pass — not computed here.
- **Risk:**
  - **R5 Disruption Risk = 5 (default)** — Layer 09 physical-infra/power/compute; not the Layer-10 agentic-AI disruption target. No change expected.
  - **R3 balance sheet:** cash $2.21B is strong, but the model is **financing-dependent** — $2.4B PP&E build in 9 months, multiple converts (2030/2029/2033) + ATM + NVIDIA equity. Dilution is real (30M new shares to NVIDIA + convert dilution). Thesis-break watch on financing/dilution stress.
  - **R1 customer concentration:** ARR now concentrated in **Microsoft (~$1.9B) + NVIDIA (~$0.7B)** — two counterparties = most of contracted ARR. Strong counterparties but high concentration; flag for R1.
  - **Bitcoin-price exposure:** mining still ~77% of revenue and being actively wound down/impaired; BTC price is a near-term earnings/cash-flow risk during the transition.
- **Rule-14 (expectations / what's priced in):** IREN is a consensus-crowded AI-infra name post-run; if/when scored, run `scripts/expectations_flag.py IREN` (P/S ≥90th pct of own range + decelerating growth) as a qualitative red-flag for the briefing — not done here. Note total revenue *declined sequentially* (Q2 $184.7M → Q3 $144.8M) even as AI-cloud grew, because mining shrank — a deceleration nuance worth surfacing.
- **Rule-15 (EPS YoY blanking):** Q3 net loss is dominated by **non-operating / one-time items** ($140.4M mining-hardware impairment + $23.7M convert capped-call unrealized loss). If EPS YoY were ever an input here, it would be a candidate for blanking per rule 15 (loss-making + one-time-dominated) — flag, with the impairment cited.
- **Tooling flag:** `sec_edgar.py IREN --forms 20-F 6-K 10-Q` missed the Q3 results 8-K and the $4.4B-ARR 8-K. IREN files **10-Q + 8-K** (domestic filer); update the form list for future pulls.
