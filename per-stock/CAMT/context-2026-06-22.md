Israeli-domiciled but USD-reporting, files 20-F/6-K (foreign filer — expect press-release 6-Ks on EDGAR, not 10-Qs). Prior to this pass it was a sub-layer pattern-match with no per-name research, and was on the R2-rubric review list. Open questions going in: is HBM order momentum still accelerating or rolling over, how concentrated is the customer base, and is the rich valuation now colliding with only mid-single-digit YoY revenue growth.

**Q1 2026 (quarter ended Mar 31, 2026), 6-K May 12, 2026:**
- Revenue **$121.7M**, +2.5% YoY vs $118.6M Q1 2025 — i.e. essentially flat (slight growth).
- GAAP gross margin **50.1%** ($60.9M); non-GAAP GM **51.0%** ($61.9M). Roughly flat vs Q1 2025 GAAP 51.0% / non-GAAP 52.1% — modest GM compression (~90bps GAAP, ~110bps non-GAAP YoY).
- GAAP operating income **$27.3M (22.4% margin)**, **down 17% YoY** vs $32.7M (27.6%). Non-GAAP op income **$31.1M (25.5%)**, also **down 17%** vs $37.3M (31.5%). → Operating-margin compression of ~520bps GAAP / ~600bps non-GAAP YoY is the standout negative.
- GAAP net income **$31.6M, diluted EPS $0.63** (down 8% YoY from $34.3M / $0.70). Non-GAAP NI **$35.3M, EPS $0.70** (down 9% from $38.7M / $0.79).
- Balance sheet: cash + deposits + marketable securities **$849.7M** (vs $851.1M Dec 31, 2025; $522.6M Mar 31, 2025) — large net-cash position, up ~$327M YoY (likely incl. a capital raise; unverified here).
- Operating cash flow **only $3.1M** in Q1 — weak vs $31.6M net income; likely working-capital build ahead of the 2H ramp (inventory/receivables for backlog). Flag: OCF/NI conversion poor this quarter; check whether it normalizes (rule-11 FCF-conversion red-flag context).

**Guidance (6-K May 12, 2026):**
- Q2 2026 revenue **$129–131M**.
- **2H26 revenue expected to grow >25% vs 1H26**, on "unprecedented" order momentum (CEO Rafi Amit). This is the key forward signal — a steep back-half ramp, not a guidance cut.

**Order announcements (6-Ks):**
- **June 2, 2026 (zk2635474.htm):** >$105M multi-system orders — **$55M from a tier-1 OSAT** (2.5D/3D AI devices) + **>$50M of Hawk systems from a leading HBM manufacturer**, all for delivery in **2027** (extends 2027 backlog).
- Q1 order color (per transcript/press, see §3/§4): leading OSATs >$90M of orders in Q1; a $31M OSAT order (CoWoS-like); a tier-1 IDM Hawk order ($25M, aggregating to $45M from that customer).

**Acquisition:** Closed acquisition of **Visual Layer** (AI/data-curation for detection/metrology/classification algorithms) — strategic, deepens AI-software capability per CEO. Size/terms not in the press release reviewed; flag for follow-up.

## 6. Implications for ratings (FLAGS ONLY — do not pre-decide)

- **D1 (sub-layer directness / AI revenue tie):** Strong positive read — ~50% of revenue explicitly AI-related + ~20% other advanced packaging; Hawk/Eagle squarely on HBM, 2.5D/3D, hybrid bonding. Among the more directly AI-levered Layer-04 names. Flag for upward review consideration vs whatever was pattern-matched.
- **D5 (durability / structural position):** Positive — entrenched in HBM/advanced-packaging inspection-metrology, new-platform traction (Hawk/Eagle doubling), 2027 backlog building. Counter-flag: HBM is cyclical; the Castellano "HBM cycle reset" view and the flat Q1 are reminders this is a capex-cycle business, not a secular annuity.
- **Growth:** Tension to surface — TTM/MRQ revenue growth is only low-single-digit YoY *now*, but the company guides >25% H2-vs-H1 and consensus models ~13% FY26 / ~19% FY27. A TTM-based growth input will understate the forward ramp (rule-9 TTM-limitation note applies — back-half-loaded year). Flag the divergence; do not pre-score.
- **Quality / margins:** Operating margin down 17% YoY (~520bps GAAP) and weak Q1 OCF ($3.1M vs $31.6M NI) — flag both for the quality/FCF-conversion red-flag check (rule 11 Step 2b). Likely opex-investment-driven, not demand; verify before treating as a quality ding.
- **Momentum (rule 11):** Refresh 50DMA % via momentum_50dma.py at any scoring pass — stock "dipped" on the print despite a beat, so price momentum may not match order momentum. Don't infer; measure.
- **Value / rule-14 expectations flag:** Run `python3 scripts/expectations_flag.py CAMT` — rich semicap multiple against flat current growth is exactly the "peak multiple, decelerating near-term growth" pattern rule 14 watches for.
