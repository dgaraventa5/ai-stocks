## 1. Pre-research mental model

Everpure (fka Pure Storage) is an enterprise all-flash storage vendor (FlashArray/FlashBlade) mid-pivot into an "Enterprise Data Cloud" platform, rebranded Feb 2026 and now adding data-intelligence software (1touch acquisition). The whole thesis hinges on a single transformative development: a DirectFlash royalty/licensing design win with one "Top-4" hyperscaler (widely reported as Meta), structured at near-100% / 75–85% gross margins, that converts the business from selling boxes to licensing IP. Going in, I expect Q1 FY2027 (quarter ended ~May 3, 2026) to show ~28% revenue growth per prior guidance, with the key open questions being (a) whether a SECOND hyperscaler has been confirmed, (b) the GAAP/non-GAAP margin gap (heavy SBC), and (c) hyperscaler customer concentration. The bear case is hyperscaler in-housing of flash controllers + single-customer dependency.

## 5. Implications for ratings (signal only — no rating numbers, no spreadsheet edits)

The 1touch/software layer is a small adjunct, not a seat-priced workflow at risk. Net: R5 = durable, default-high; this is a beneficiary of AI, not a target of it.
