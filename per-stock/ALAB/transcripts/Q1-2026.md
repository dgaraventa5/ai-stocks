## CEO Prepared Remarks (Jitendra Mohan)

### Revenue and Growth
- Q1 revenue of $308.4M, up 14% sequentially and 93% year-over-year
- Beat consensus of $292.2M by 5.5%
- Fifth consecutive quarter of beats
- "We have now shipped millions of PCIe Gen 6 ports to date"
- PCIe Gen 6 contributed over one-third of total company revenue for the first time

### Scorpio Platform
- **"Scorpio will become our largest product line by the end of the year"** — this is the single most important forward-looking statement on the call
- Scorpio X-Series (scale-up): now supports up to 320 lanes with hardware-accelerated Hypercast and in-network compute engines, boosting collective AI operations by up to 2x
- Scorpio P-Series (scale-out): spans 32-320 lanes for diverse system topologies
- Initial volume shipments of Scorpio X 320-lane began in Q1; production ramp expected throughout 2026
- Two additional major hyperscalers expected to receive Scorpio P-Series by late 2026
- Scorpio X-Series revenue projected to exceed P-Series revenue
- Scorpio was ~15% of revenue in 2025; needs to approach 25%+ to become largest product line by year-end

### Signal Conditioning (Aries + Taurus)
- Aries PCIe/CXL retimers: strong PCIe 6 adoption across both scale-out and scale-up platforms
- Taurus Ethernet AEC (Active Electrical Cables): continued strong deployment across GPU, XPU, and general-purpose systems
- Margin improvement in Q1 partly attributable to lower mix of hardware sales within signal conditioning

### Leo CXL Memory Controller
- On track for early volume ramp with Microsoft Azure M-series virtual machines
- Secured new custom design win for KV cache offload application, shipments expected 2027

### Optical Interconnect Strategy
Three-pronged approach via XScale Photonics acquisition (fully integrated, Israel Design Center established):
1. **High-density detachable fiber-attach solutions** — qualification progressing at a major AI platform provider; volume shipments targeted 2027
2. **Near-Package Optics (NPO) chipsets** — enabling multi-rack AI clusters from 2027
3. **Co-Packaged Optics (CPO) enabled Scorpio X switches** — supporting larger domains, 2028+ timeframe

### Custom Solutions Business
- NVLink Fusion custom switching products in deep development with NVIDIA and a hyperscaler for hybrid racks — revenue expected 2027
- Custom KV cache design win using Leo CXL controller — revenue expected 2027
- Management framed custom solutions as "multibillion-dollar market opportunity"
- Emphasized "systematic, thoughtful evaluation" of custom opportunities to protect margins

### UALink
- Initial UALink-based switch deployments planned for 2027
- Timed to intercept Amazon and AMD ASIC/GPU launches in 2027
- Management emphasized "complexity and speed increase" in next-gen devices supports substantially higher ASPs than PCIe switches

### Market Commentary
- Acceleration in AI infrastructure spending across hyperscalers, AI labs, and sovereign entities — "early-stage market buildout"
- Shift from training to inference creating new opportunities (KV cache offload, agentic computing)
- Silicon dollar content opportunity "beyond $1,000 per XPU within AI racks"
- Company embedded in "nearly 90% of global AI computing servers"

## Analyst Q&A — Key Themes

### Scorpio Ramp Trajectory
- Scorpio X 320-lane initial volumes began Q1; production ramp H2 2026
- Two new hyperscaler Scorpio P-Series engagements for late 2026
- X-Series revenue expected to exceed P-Series
- Path from ~15% of revenue (2025) to largest product line (year-end 2026)
- 320-lane switch to be demonstrated live at Computex 2026 (June 2-5)

### Gross Margin Trajectory
- Q2 guide of ~73% includes ~200bps Amazon warrant (noncash) + ~140bps Scorpio hardware mix
- Management framed the Amazon warrant as "strategic partnership" — accounting treatment "misrepresents true operational performance"
- Underlying margin trajectory depends on Scorpio semiconductor vs. hardware content mix
- Signal conditioning margins improved as hardware share declined within the segment

### Customer Diversification
- Broadening engagement beyond initial hyperscaler base
- Two additional major hyperscalers for Scorpio P-Series late 2026
- Custom solutions business diversifies revenue streams (NVLink Fusion, KV cache)
- Sovereign AI entity engagement noted

### Competitive Position
- Positioned as de-facto standard in PCIe 6/CXL for AI clusters
- Hardware-accelerated Hypercast = differentiated IP vs. generic switches
- In-network compute offloading is structural advantage
- Competing against Broadcom in fabric switching (different challenge than retimers)
- Filing notes competition in "increasingly crowded" cloud connectivity solutions market

### Capital Allocation
- $1.18B cash position provides ample runway
- XScale Photonics acquisition payments still flowing
- No buyback or dividend discussed
- R&D investment continues to ramp ($96.2M in Q1)
