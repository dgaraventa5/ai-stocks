## 1. Pre-research mental model (carried forward from 2026-05-25)

- Q3 FY2026: $407M revenue (+201.5% YoY, +51.9% QoQ)

- DustPhotonics acquisition announced April 2026 for ~$750M+ — expected to close Q1 FY2027
- Five hyperscaler customers; three each >10% of revenue
- All major patent disputes settled (Amphenol, Molex, TE Connectivity)
- Q4 earnings scheduled June 1, 2026

## 3. Key findings since prior context

### Finding 1: Gross margin compression did NOT materialize
The primary concern from 2026-05-25 was Q4 guidance of 64-66% non-GAAP GM vs. 68.6% in Q3. Actual Q4 came in at 68.3% — the compression never happened. This eliminates what was the primary "yellow flag" from the prior analysis. The quality/margin story for CRDO is intact.
- Source: TradingView/ChartMill Q4 earnings coverage (2026-06-01)

### Finding 2: "Inline guidance" sell-off is a market dynamic, not a thesis problem
Q1 FY2027 midpoint of $470M exactly matched analyst consensus. Despite beating Q4 on both revenue and EPS, the stock sold off because it failed to beat consensus on forward guidance. This is a valuation/sentiment phenomenon — the underlying business continues growing at 150%+ YoY and has exceptional operating leverage.
- Source: ChartMill "Q4 Results Beat Estimates but Inline Guidance Triggers Post-Earnings Sell-Off" (2026-06-01)

### Finding 3: FY2027 is an optical story
The company's growth narrative for FY2027 shifts from AEC-driven to optical-driven in H2. >$600M optical revenue target includes DustPhotonics SiPho PICs + existing ZeroFlap + Optical DSPs. This is a major product portfolio expansion that, if it executes, could sustain 50%+ YoY growth through FY2027.
- Source: Yahoo Finance Q4 Earnings Call Highlights (2026-06-01)

### Finding 4: CEO compensation signals management conviction
StockTitan noted the company "grants ambitious CEO performance PSU award" — a performance-contingent compensation award. While not a standalone catalyst, it aligns management with shareholders at high growth targets.
- Source: StockTitan (2026-06-01)

### Finding 5: EPS beat streak continues — analysts systematically underestimate CRDO
Q4 EPS of $1.16 beat $0.98 consensus by 18.4%. Pattern: Q1 FY2026 beat by 44%, Q3 FY2026 beat by significant margin. The operating leverage in a fabless model at this scale is being consistently undermodeled.
- Source: Zacks/search synthesis
