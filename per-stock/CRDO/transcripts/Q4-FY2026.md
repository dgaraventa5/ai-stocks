## Key Beat vs. Prior Guide

Prior Q4 guidance: revenue $425–435M (mid $430M), non-GAAP GM **64–66%**.

Actual: Revenue $437M (+$7M vs. midguide), non-GAAP GM **68.3%** — **230–430bps above guide**.

The gross margin beat is the most significant surprise. Management had guided a meaningful step-down in Q4; instead, margins held near Q3 levels. This resolves the "pricing power yellow flag" raised in the May 25 context briefing. No explanation was given in available summaries for why Q4 margins beat the guide — likely higher-than-expected AEC mix with better-than-modeled pricing terms.

## Management Prepared Remarks (Summary)

### CEO William Brennan

- Opened by calling FY2026 "a defining year" — revenue tripled to $1.3B, non-GAAP NI grew 5×, operating margin expanded 2,144bps.
- AEC business remains the core growth engine. Described AI cluster connectivity demand as "fundamentally multi-year" with no near-term signs of capex slowdown at hyperscaler customers.
- Five hyperscaler customers engaged; three each exceeded 10% of revenue in Q4 (FY26 annual shows two dominate: Customer B ~33%, Customer D ~32%).
- Described FY27 as "the inflection point for Credo's optical business." Three optical legs (optical DSPs + SiPho PICs via DustPhotonics + ZeroFlap transceivers) each expected to exceed $100M in FY27, totaling >$600M.
- DustPhotonics closed May 28 (just before fiscal year-end); integration underway. Described as "vertically integrating the optical stack" from SerDes IP through silicon photonics die through the finished transceiver.
- ALCs (Active LED Cables, 30m reach) sampling in FY27, revenue in FY28 — bridges AEC (7m) and full optical.
- CEO awarded 100% performance-based PSU: 6 tranches with revenue hurdles $2.5B → $7.5B and stock price hurdles $244.70 → $489.40 through June 2030 — management compensation fully aligned with long-term revenue scaling.

### CFO Dan Fleming

- Described supply chain as "secured for planned ramps and upside" — wafers and packaging across process nodes locked in. (Contrast with earlier call commentary that mentioned "tightness" — management resolved the bottlenecks entering FY27.)
- FY26 operating expense growth was "considerably slower than revenue," producing massive operating leverage (2,144bps expansion).
- R&D investment will accelerate in FY27 as DustPhotonics integration adds headcount and the optical roadmap requires additional NRE.

## DustPhotonics Integration

Closed May 28, 2026 (within FY26; originally guided Q2 calendar 2026).
- SiPho PIC portfolio: 400G/800G/1.6T, roadmap to 3.2T
- Use cases: NPO (Near-Package Optics) and CPO (Co-Packaged Optics) — positions Credo for 3.2T+
- Combined optical revenue target raised from >$500M (pre-DustPhotonics) to >$600M for FY27
- Expected to reduce supply dependence, lower bill-of-materials at scale
- Non-GAAP EPS accretive in FY27
