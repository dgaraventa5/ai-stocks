## CEO Commentary: Strategic Vision (Bill Brennan)

### On FY2026 performance

[Paraphrase] Management highlighted that FY2026 was a transformational year — revenue tripled, non-GAAP net income grew more than 5x, and the company moved from a narrow AEC supplier to a multi-product connectivity platform.

### On DustPhotonics acquisition

- Expected to close Q2 calendar 2026 (= Q1 FY2027, May–August 2026 timeframe)
- Combined portfolio: ZeroFlap Optical Transceivers + Optical DSPs + Silicon Photonics PICs
- Expected to generate >$600M in combined optical revenue in FY2027 (some sources: >$500M)
- Optical DSPs, SiPho PICs, and ZeroFlap each expected to grow >80% in FY2027
- Rationale: Positions Credo across 800G, 1.6T, and future 3.2T for NPO (Near-Package Optics) and CPO (Co-Packaged Optics) architectures

### On AEC business

- AECs remain the core revenue driver — hyperscaler deployments for GPU-to-GPU interconnect in AI training clusters
- Management committed to disciplined OpEx growth as optical ramp accelerates R&D spend

### On customer concentration

- Three hyperscalers each >10% of revenue in Q4
- Fourth hyperscaler approaching 10%; fifth secured
- Customer diversification improving
