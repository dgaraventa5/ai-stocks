## 1. Pre-research mental model (verbatim, written before research)

> ProPetro is a Permian-focused pressure pumping (hydraulic fracturing) oilfield services company, historically tied heavily to a few large E&P customers — Pioneer Natural Resources was the anchor (~50%+ of revenue historically), and Pioneer was acquired by ExxonMobil, so PUMP's biggest customer is now Exxon. This is a cyclical, capital-intensive, commoditized frac-services business with weak pricing power. My training has them transitioning their fleet to electric/Tier-4 dual-fuel ("FORCE" e-fleets) to win dedicated contracts. The "AI angle" is a recent pivot: PROPWR, a mobile/distributed power-generation business (natural gas gensets) that could serve datacenters and other off-grid power needs — but as of my training I have little/no evidence of contracted datacenter MW; it looked early-stage and largely aspirational. Frac is deeply off-thesis for AI infrastructure; PROPWR is the only thread, and I suspect it's thin. Most recent quarter in my training is roughly Q3/Q4 2025.

## 4. Layer assignment

ProPetro is **not a clean fit** for any AI-supply-chain layer. The legacy business — Permian pressure pumping / frac services — is **off-thesis** (it serves oil & gas E&P, not AI infrastructure). The only AI thread is **PROPWR**, a distributed on-site natural-gas + BESS power business now winning datacenter contracts. That maps to **Layer 1 — Power Generation → Distributed / on-site power** (peers in map: Bloom Energy, Cummins, Generac, GE Vernova). Proposed label: **`01-power-generation`** (distributed/on-site power, PROPWR datacenter gensets).

**Off-thesis flag:** As of Q1 2026, PROPWR is **<1% of revenue** ($2.2M of $270.7M) and EBITDA-negative. The AI exposure is a contracted *future* option (240 MW signed, one 60 MW datacenter deal installing), not current AI revenue. Recommend the row carry a note that PUMP is **option-on-pivot, not an established AI-infrastructure name** — the score should not treat it as a power-gen pure-play. If the orchestrator's thesis gate requires established AI revenue, PUMP is a watch-the-pivot name, not yet a thesis name.
