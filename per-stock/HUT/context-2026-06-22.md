## 1. Pre-research mental model

HUT was a pure bitcoin miner now executing an aggressive AI/HPC-landlord pivot, backed by a large secured-power portfolio. Prior mental model (capacity-mw.json as-of 2026-05-06): 1,350 secured gross MW (1,020 under management + 330 under construction), with a 352 MW TX lease and a ~1,230 MW "under development" tranche excluded as speculative. It controls ~80% of American Bitcoin (ABTC), a Trump-family-associated vehicle, which adds related-party/governance optics. Key open questions: how much of the AI pipeline has converted to signed take-or-pay leases (Value/MW vs. AI-Thesis split per rule 13), and whether the pivot is being financed without crushing equity dilution. SALP fully exited its $40M equity stake in Q1 2026 (per news-log) — a notable bear signal to weigh.

## 2. Latest filing highlights

Lead items: secured MW / contracted leases, then financing, ABTC, bitcoin, P&L.

**Contracted AI capacity (the rule-13 Value object) — materially advanced since prior model:**
- **$16.8B total contracted, triple-net, take-or-pay lease revenue across two hyperscale AI campuses, 597 MW IT capacity** (Q1 2026 results PR, 8-K filed 2026-05-06, hut-20260506xex99d1.htm). This is the headline change — prior model treated most of this as "development."
  - **River Bend (Louisiana):** 15-year, **245 MW IT** lease with Fluidstack, **$7.0B** base-term value; supported by ~330 MW utility power; first data hall commissioning **Q2 2027**. Part of the Anthropic/Fluidstack partnership (Dec 17 2025) for "at least 245 MW up to 2.295 GW"; Google backstops the Fluidstack obligations (DCD; PRNewswire 302644600). ROFO for +1,000 MW IT at future phases.
  - **Beacon Point (Nueces County, TX, ~Corpus Christi):** 15-year, **352 MW IT** lease, **$9.8B** base-term value, tenant rated **AA- or higher** (high investment grade), on ~521-acre property (8-K 2026-06-04 Item 7.01; DCD "352MW Texas IG tenant").
- **Development pipeline (excluded from secured per rule-13 methodology):** 8,375 MW total as of 2026-05-06 — 5,315 MW under diligence, 1,680 MW under exclusivity, 550 MW under development, **830 MW under construction** (Q1 2026 earnings call, Insider Monkey transcript). Note: the under-construction figure (830 MW) and under-development (550 MW) bracket the prior json's 330+1,230 split — see capacity-mw.json reconciliation flag in §5/§6.

**Financing (capital-structure de-risking, project-level, non-recourse to parent):**
- **Beacon Point DC LLC** (wholly-owned indirect sub) priced **$4.25B 6.129% Senior Secured Notes due 2042** on 2026-06-04 (8-K 2026-06-05 Item 8.01); closed ~2026-06-09/06-10 (8-K 2026-06-10). Proceeds fund the 352 MW Nueces TX turnkey six-hall DC + substation, leased to the AA- tenant. Notes are 144A/Reg S, secured by first-priority liens, amortizing semi-annually from 2028 (one search returned a 6.192% / Nov-15-2042 description — minor coupon discrepancy to confirm against the indenture; the 8-K pricing PR states 6.129%).
- **River Bend financing (prior):** $3.25B fully-amortizing 16.5-yr IG senior secured notes (Q1 PR). Reported terms: loan-to-cost raised ~85%→95%, **returned $184M of equity to Hut 8 at closing, no incremental Hut 8 equity contribution expected** (search of 8-K financing exhibits) — i.e., the landlord build is being financed at the project level, limiting parent dilution.
- **$150M Coatue convertible note** is "substantially in the money" and subject to mandatory redemption beginning late Q2 2026; assuming conversion, company states it will have "no meaningful recourse debt at the parent level" (10-Q FY2026). Conversion is dilutive — flag for Risk.

**American Bitcoin (ABTC) — related party:**
- HUT holds **585,779,924 Class B shares ≈ 80% voting power** as of 2026-04-10 (ABTC DEF 14A; remaining ~20% incl. Eric Trump & Donald Trump Jr.). ABTC consolidated into HUT financials.
- ABTC held **~6,899–7,021 BTC** (≈$489–491M Bitcoin+cash at Q1) and scaled to **>28 EH/s** (~8 months public); 16th-largest public BTC holder (CoinDesk Mar 18 2026; earnings call).
- ABTC seeking authority for a **1-for-5 to 1-for-40 reverse split** at its 2026 annual meeting (ABTC DEF 14A) — governance/optics flag.

**Bitcoin held (consolidated):** ~**16,332 BTC** at 2026-03-31 (≈9,311 HUT + ≈7,021 ABTC) (earnings call). ~$1.3B cash + Bitcoin liquidity at Q1 (Q1 PR).

**P&L (Q1 2026, three months ended 2026-03-31, Q1 PR / 10-Q):**
- Revenue **$71.0M** (+226% YoY vs $21.8M): Compute $66.0M, Power $3.7M, Digital Infrastructure $1.3M.
- Gross margin **~64%** (vs ~14% PY) — call.
- Net loss **$(253.1)M** (vs $(134.3)M PY), driven by **$295.7M primarily unrealized losses on digital assets** (mark-to-market on BTC, incl. consolidated ABTC).
- Adjusted EBITDA **$(250.5)M** — also digital-asset-MTM-dominated.

**Governance:** 2026 Annual Meeting held 2026-06-11; directors (incl. CEO Asher Genoot) re-elected; 83,316,655 shares represented (8-K 2026-06-12 Item 5.07). No adverse outcome.

## 6. Implications for ratings

Flags only — no rating decisions made here. R5 defaults to 5 (HUT is Layer 09, not Layer 10).

- **Rule-13 EV/MW Value object (CRITICAL):** capacity-mw.json HUT = 1,350 secured gross MW, asof 2026-05-06, basis "1,020 under management + 330 under construction; 352 MW TX in development." This basis is now **internally inconsistent with the company's own Q1 disclosure**: (a) Beacon Point 352 MW IT is now a *signed, financed* lease, not "development" — it should likely move into secured; (b) River Bend 245 MW IT is a signed lease (330 MW utility power) and should be checked for inclusion; (c) the transcript's pipeline buckets are 830 MW under construction + 550 MW under development (not the json's 330+1,230). **Recommend re-deriving HUT secured gross MW before trusting EV/MW.** Note the IT-vs-gross unit issue: River Bend 245 MW IT ≈ 330 MW gross (utility); Beacon Point 352 MW is stated as *critical IT* (→ ×1.25 ≈ 440 MW gross-equiv per the json convention). A defensible secured-gross figure of energized + signed-and-financed (River Bend ~330 gross + Beacon Point ~440 gross-equiv + existing energized mining capacity) could differ from 1,350 — **do NOT auto-update; flag for Dom to set the basis.** Per rule 13, leave AI-conversion judgment out of this number.
- **AI Thesis (D1–D5):** This is where the AI-conversion quality lives (rule-13 division of labor). Strong positives: $16.8B contracted take-or-pay, Anthropic partnership (up to 2.295 GW), Google backstop, AA-+ and NVIDIA-spec tenants. D1/D2/D5 plausibly move up vs. a pre-conversion rating. Counterweight: revenue starts Q2 2027 (execution/timing risk); pipeline beyond 597 MW is unsecured.
- **Growth:** Revenue +226% YoY is real and operational (compute segment). EPS YoY is **one-time/MTM-dominated** ($295.7M unrealized BTC loss) — per **rule 15**, EPS YoY input is a strong candidate to **blank** (cite the MTM item) so Growth averages the revenue metrics. Flag for the rater.
- **Momentum (M / 50DMA%):** Stock spiked +30–37% on Beacon Point and ~25% on Anthropic news; Rosenblatt Buy $124 PT. Refresh 50DMA% via `momentum_50dma.py` at scoring time.
- **Risk (R1–R5):**
  - *R3 balance sheet / financing:* materially improved — project-level non-recourse notes, $184M equity returned, post-Coatue-conversion "no meaningful recourse parent debt." But **Coatue $150M convert mandatory redemption late Q2 2026 is dilutive**; weigh.
  - *Bitcoin-price exposure:* ~16,332 BTC means earnings and book value swing hard with BTC (Q1 $295.7M MTM loss). Structural risk to the equity even as the landlord business de-risks.
  - *Related-party / governance (ABTC):* ~80%-owned Trump-family-linked subsidiary, consolidated; pending 1:5–1:40 reverse split. Governance/optics + consolidation-noise risk.
  - *R5 (Disruption):* defaults **5** (physical power/DC infra, not Layer 10).
- **Rule-14 expectations red flag:** Run `python3 scripts/expectations_flag.py HUT` at scoring time. Caution: HUT's revenue base is tiny ($71M) vs. a multi-billion contracted backlog and a BTC-distorted P&L — P/S and growth-percentile signals may be noisy; interpret qualitatively.
- **Bear datapoint to weigh (not a rating input):** SALP fully exited its $40M HUT equity stake in Q1 2026 (news-log 2026-06-12).

**THESIS-BREAK watch — status:** No break triggered. AI-pivot is *advancing* (597 MW signed, financed), not slipping. Financing/dilution stress is *easing* at parent level (watch Coatue redemption + any future ATM/equity). Bitcoin-price exposure and ABTC related-party/governance remain live risks but are unchanged in kind. Re-examine if: River Bend/Beacon Point commissioning slips materially past Q2 2027; a large dilutive equity raise appears; or BTC drawdown forces asset sales.
