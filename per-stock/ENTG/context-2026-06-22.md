## 1. Pre-research mental model

ENTG is a Layer-04 specialty-materials and contamination-control supplier (deposition materials, CMP slurries, formulated etch/clean, liquid/gas filtration, fluid handling) selling consumables into wafer fabs — a "picks-and-shovels-of-the-picks-and-shovels" content-per-wafer story whose revenue scales with wafer starts and node complexity rather than lumpy WFE capex. Two structural overhangs: (1) a heavy debt load from the 2022 CMC Materials acquisition, and (2) China export-control exposure (both direct revenue and product-localization risk). The AI angle is indirect-but-real: more advanced logic/HBM/3D-NAND complexity means more materials intensity per wafer. Expectation going in: a quality compounder with a deleveraging clock running and a China tail risk, trading on a recovery in fab utilization.

- **D1 (end-market growth/AI directness):** Indirect AI exposure confirmed and improving — advanced logic 40% / memory 30% tied to AI compute and HBM/3D-NAND complexity; content-per-wafer double-digit growth narrative. - **D2 (competitive position / new wins):** Positions-of-record at new nodes (advanced logic, 3D NAND, DRAM); moly/selective-etch/CMP/filtration wins. Advanced packaging admitted "limited" (<$100M+ run-rate, prior underinvestment) — a relative gap vs peers.
- **D3/D4:** No material change surfaced this pass.
- **D5 (moat):** Materials qualification/switching-cost moat intact; specialty consumables. No change flagged.
- **Momentum (M / 50DMA per rule 11):** Two-quarter beat-and-raise cadence, constructive order commentary. Refresh objective 50DMA% via `scripts/momentum_50dma.py` before any Momentum rating change (not done in this pass).
- **R3 (balance sheet / leverage):** **Primary watch.** Net leverage 3.6x, net debt ~$3.2B (LT debt $3,651.2M − cash $442.7M); only $400M variable-rate. Glide path to ~3.0x by end-2026 with $50M/qtr paydown and $144M/qtr FCF — improving but still elevated leverage. R3 should reflect "high-but-deleveraging," not distress.
- **R (geography — China):** **Watch.** Export-control overhang materially de-risked operationally (85%→90% in-region) but not eliminated; further restrictions on advanced materials remain a structural risk with no quantified at-risk disclosure. Keep China as an active geography risk input.
