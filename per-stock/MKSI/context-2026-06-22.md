## 1. Pre-research mental model

MKSI is a Layer-04 semi-equipment subsystems & instruments supplier (vacuum, lasers/photonics, RF power, motion) feeding WFE, plus the Atotech electronics/specialty-chemicals business (PCB/advanced-packaging plating) acquired in 2022. The AI angle is two-pronged: WFE content per tool, and advanced-packaging plating chemistry that benefits as AI accelerators drive complex substrate/PCB content. The defining risk is the post-Atotech balance sheet — the deal loaded ~$4B+ of debt, so leverage/covenant capacity and the pace of deleveraging are the key watch (R3). Entering this pass I assumed mid-cycle WFE recovery with leverage still elevated but coming down.

- **M (Momentum):** Strong beat + raise + stock surge; refresh objective **50DMA %** via `momentum_50dma.py` at next scoring pass (rule 11). Subjective EPS-revisions/RS likely positive.
- **R3 (balance sheet) — KEY RISK, flag HIGH LEVERAGE:** Net leverage **3.5x** is still elevated (this remains the single most important watch on MKSI), BUT the trajectory is clearly improving: >$1B repaid since 2024, refi extended maturities (2034 notes) and cut interest ~$27M/yr, explicit 2.0–2.5x target by YE2026/early-2027, $1.5B liquidity, no covenant stress. R3 should reflect *elevated-but-deleveraging*, not distress. Watch the term-loan paydown cadence each quarter.
- **R5 (Disruption Risk):** Defaults to **5** (non-Layer-10 physical semi-equipment/chemistry infrastructure; no credible agentic-AI disruption to the revenue model). Per rule 16.
- **R1/R2/R4:** No new adverse data this pass (no customer-concentration, geography, or regulatory shock surfaced). R4 minor: tariff drag 30–40bps, neutralized.
