## 1. Pre-research mental model (verbatim, written before any data was pulled)

> "Teradyne's Semi Test segment (the bulk of revenue) benefits from AI structurally: HBM stacks and large AI logic/chiplet devices need more test seconds per die, and system-level test (SLT) attach is rising. However, Advantest — not Teradyne — has historically led in memory/HBM test (entrenched at SK Hynix and in Nvidia GPU SoC test with the V93000), while Teradyne's strength is mobile SoC (Apple/Qualcomm via UltraFLEXplus) plus growing 'VIP' custom-ASIC compute wins (e.g., Broadcom-designed hyperscaler TPUs). Robotics (Universal Robots + MiR) has been a multi-year drag with weak industrial demand and restructuring. My training reliably covers through Q3 2025 (reported late Oct 2025) — Teradyne was guiding 2026 as an AI-compute-and-memory-driven growth year; Q4 2025 and Q1 2026 results are at-or-past my cutoff, so beat/miss, bookings, and any HBM4 win/loss news from 2026 are exactly what this pass must establish."
