## 1. Pre-research mental model

GLW is a Layer 07 specialty-glass/optical-materials conglomerate whose AI driver is the Optical Communications segment (optical fiber + cable + connectivity for AI data centers, plus new GenAI fiber/connector products). The bull case is that hyperscaler AI buildout is pulling enormous fiber + connectivity demand, and Corning's "Springboard" plan packages that into a credible multi-year run-rate ramp. The complication for an AI-pure rating is segment mix: Display, Specialty Materials, Environmental, Life Sciences, and Solar dilute the AI signal, so D1 (AI revenue mix) is blended, not pure-play. Going in I expected strong Optical growth and a raised Springboard target; the open questions were the exact Optical growth rate, margin trajectory, and whether order/demand momentum is decelerating.

## 6. Implications for ratings (flags only — do NOT pre-decide)

- **D1 (AI revenue directness/mix):** Optical is the AI engine (+36%, ~$1.8B/qtr) but Corning is a multi-segment conglomerate (Display, Specialty Materials, Environmental, Life Sciences, Solar). D1 should stay **blended**, not pure-play — flag for review of whether the current rating reflects the AI-mix share rather than the whole company.
- **D2 (customer/contract traction):** Materially strengthened — Meta up-to-$6B, two more unnamed hyperscalers, NVIDIA up-to-$3.2B partnership + 10x capacity. Flag for review (likely upward).
- **D5 (durability of AI demand / order visibility):** Long-term hyperscaler agreements with shared investment risk + extended Springboard ($40B/2030) give multi-year visibility. Flag for review. R5 defaults to **5** (non-Layer-10 physical infra / optical materials — no agentic-AI disruption to the revenue model).
- **Momentum (M):** Strong beat, raised guide, no demand softening, NVIDIA/Meta catalysts in the quarter. Refresh `momentum_50dma.py` at scoring time; subjective EPS-revisions/relative-strength flags likely positive.
- **R1–R4 (Risk):** Customer concentration rising as hyperscaler deals grow (R1 watch) but partially de-risked by long-term contracts; broad non-AI segment base cushions cyclicality. China/export exposure (Display, glass) — re-check at scoring. R5 = 5 (default, non-Layer-10).
