## 1. Pre-research mental model

Vertiv is a leveraged, near pure-play beneficiary of the AI data-center buildout: it sells the power (UPS, busway, switchgear) and thermal (air + liquid cooling, CDUs, trim/dry coolers) that GPU racks need. The thesis rests on (a) a huge AI-driven order backlog (~$15B-ish) and book-to-bill well above 1, (b) a tight strategic alignment with NVIDIA's rack roadmap — including the move to 800VDC power and liquid cooling as rack densities blow past 100kW+, and (c) margin expansion as scale and mix improve. The main risk is valuation: this is a consensus AI long that has re-rated hard, so the bar (what's priced in) is high — any order/backlog deceleration or hyperscaler capex wobble would hit a stretched multiple. I expect Q1 2026 to show continued strong organic growth and a raised FY26 guide, with the watch-item being order/backlog momentum sequentially.
