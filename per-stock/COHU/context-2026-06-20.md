## 1. Pre-research mental model (verbatim, Step 1)

> **Cohu (COHU)** — back-end semiconductor test handler maker. I believe: makes test handlers (pick-and-place, gravity, turret), thermal subsystems, contactors/sockets, and inspection/metrology equipment; acquired Xcerra in 2018 (which brought the LTX-Credence semiconductor ATE and the Multitest/ECT handler+contactor lines) and Ismeca. Heavy exposure to automotive and industrial end markets (analog/power/MCU test), which means it's been in a cyclical downturn through 2024-2025 as auto/industrial semis destocked. Recurring revenue (consumables: contactors, spares, services) is a meaningful and counter-cyclical-ish portion (~ low-to-mid 40s % of sales, management's stated goal). Limited direct AI/HBM/compute-test exposure relative to Teradyne/Advantest, which dominate the SoC/HBM ATE used for AI accelerators — Cohu is more of a handler/interface play. Most recent quarter per my training is probably somewhere in 2024. Smallish cap (~$1.5-2B). Net cash balance sheet historically.

- **ANOMALY / cyclical trough (affects Growth + Quality, main-loop attention required):** FY2024 and FY2025 are **GAAP net losses** (−$69.8M, −$74.3M); operating margin, ROIC, net margin, and **EPS YoY are negative or n/m**. TTM through Q1'26 is still loss-making (Q1'26 net loss −$12.1M). **GAAP EPS YoY and margin-based Quality inputs will score garbage on trough data and understate the forward business.** This is exactly the staleness/low-base trap rules 9 & 15 warn about. *I am NOT blanking anything* — flagging for the main loop: consider whether the rule-15 EPS-YoY-blank applies (here the cause is **cyclical operating losses**, not a one-time non-operating item, so rule-15 may **not** strictly apply — rule 15 is for one-time-*dominated* changes; a clean cyclical loss arguably should stay and just score low, with a low-base caveat). Gross margin (46%) is healthy and *is* a usable Quality input. Growth on **revenue** is genuinely positive (+12.7% FY25, +29% Q1'26 YoY) even though EPS is not.
- **Value:** multiple at 3-year high on trough earnings; forward P/E ~117x, EV/S ~6.5x. Value inputs (Fwd P/E, P/S, PEG, FCF yield) will likely score **low** — correctly, the name is priced for recovery. FCF yield is thin (TTM FCF ~$11-15M on ~$3.5B cap).
- **AI Thesis (D1-D5):** the big upgrade vs prior. HPC/AI now ~$80-100M of ~$510-545M FY26 revenue (~15-19% AI-tied, trending up) — D1 likely a **3**. Hyperscaler exposure (D5): exposure is **indirect** (sells to IDMs/OSATs/foundries who serve the AI buildout; no customer >10%, hyperscalers not direct customers) — likely **2-3**.
