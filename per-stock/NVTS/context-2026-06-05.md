The valuation is even more extreme than I assumed.

## 5. DIFF — what changed vs my training

- **Revenue scale much smaller than I thought:** ~$8.6M/qtr (~$34-42M annualized), not ~$80M. Legacy base is shrinking, not flat.
- **NVIDIA partnership confirmed REAL** (formal 2025-05-21 selection) but **explicitly not a 2026 revenue event** — inflection is 2027. Roughly all of the ~$6-7B market cap is priced on a future design-in: at ~$42M 2026E revenue, EV/S ≈ ~160x; current revenue supports a tiny fraction of the valuation.
- **NVIDIA selected MULTIPLE partners** (TI, Renesas, Infineon, Navitas) for 800V HVDC — Navitas is NOT sole-source. This caps the moat/pricing-power story.
- **Heavy, ongoing dilution:** $122M ATM completed, $250M shelf + $125M ATM live, 3.28M earn-out shares issued Jun 2026, Live Oak settlement shares. Share count rising 230M → 234M+ and climbing. Cash cushion rebuilt to ~$221M (good runway vs ~$16M/qtr burn) but at shareholders' expense.
- **GAAP gross margin negative (-9.3%)** due to amortization/inventory; non-GAAP GM ~39% is the operating reality.

## 6. Implications for ratings (flags only — not pre-deciding)

- **D1 AI Revenue %:** High-power/AI now "majority" of a tiny revenue base — but "high power" bundles grid/industrial, not purely AI-infra. The AI-*specific* % is smaller and the absolute $ is trivial. Forward-looking, not current.
- **D2 Position / D3 Moat:** GaN/SiC power is mid-chain with multiple credible competitors (Infineon, TI, Renesas, Power Integrations, EPC, onsemi/Wolfspeed on SiC). NVIDIA multi-sourced the 800V design — pricing power unproven. Lean LOW.
- **D4 Capacity:** Fabless/asset-light (TSMC GaN, third-party SiC). Not a capex-buildout story — neutral.
- **D5 Hyperscaler exposure:** Indirect — sells into NVIDIA's reference design, not directly to hyperscalers, and not yet at revenue. Low-to-indirect.
- **M1 EPS revisions:** Consensus revenue revised UP (2026 +12%, 2027 +10%, 2028 +20%) on NVIDIA narrative, but still deeply loss-making. Mixed-positive on revisions.
- **M2 Relative strength:** Extreme outperformance (+262% YTD) vs SOXX/SMH — top of band.
- **M3 Insider:** Only sells (CEO open-market 13.3k sh) + a gift; NO buys. Rubric → routine sells, no buying = baseline 3.
- **R1 Customer concentration:** Disclosed risk; consumer base diffuse but future AI revenue concentrates on the NVIDIA ecosystem. Flag.
- **R2 Geo risk:** Meaningful Asia/China consumer revenue (the part that's declining); fabless supply via TSMC (Taiwan). Moderate.
- **R3 Balance-sheet risk:** Net cash (~$221M, no major debt) BUT persistent ~$16M/qtr burn funded by serial dilution. Cash-rich today, structurally dilutive.
- **R4 Regulatory/litigation:** Live Oak/SPAC settlement, earn-out share disputes — housekeeping, not existential.

**Thesis-break / speculative-risk candor:** This is a story stock. ~$6-7B cap on ~$8.6M/qtr revenue (declining YoY) = the entire valuation is an option on the 2027+ NVIDIA 800V ramp materializing AND Navitas capturing meaningful socketed content against TI/Infineon/Renesas. Thesis breaks if: (a) NVIDIA's 800V/Rubin timeline slips or Navitas content gets designed out; (b) 2027 inflection fails to show in bookings by late-2026; (c) dilution continues without revenue follow-through. The non-AI base is actively eroding, so there is no fundamental floor under the multiple.
