## ⚠️ Material Events

**NVDA** — Q1 FY2027 earnings (8-K, 2026-05-20, Item 2.02). Revenue $81.6B (+85% YoY, +20% QoQ), record quarter. Data center $75.2B (+92% YoY). Non-GAAP GM 75.0%. Guided Q2 revenue $91.0B ±2%. Networking revenue $14.8B (+199% YoY) — major signal for upstream optical/interconnect names. Hyperscale and non-hyperscale now split 50/50, indicating demand broadening beyond the big four. China: zero data center compute revenue, excluded from guidance. FCF $48.6B for the quarter. Raised dividend from $0.01 to $0.25/share, authorized additional $80B buyback.

**D / NEE** — Dominion/NextEra merger disclosure (D 8-K, 2026-05-22, Item 8.01; NEE 8-K, 2026-05-26, Item 8.01). Dominion filed merger risk factors referencing the May 15, 2026 all-stock merger agreement with NextEra. If completed, creates the largest US utility and combines NextEra's renewable development capability with Dominion's Northern Virginia data center territory — the world's largest data center market. Regulatory approvals (FERC, NRC, VA SCC, NC UC) expected to take 18-24 months. This is arguably the most consequential single event for Layer 1 of the AI supply chain this year.

**IREN** — $1.6B GPU supply agreement with Dell (8-K, 2026-05-26, Items 1.01, 7.01). Subsidiary signed agreement to procure GPUs for deployment at Childress, TX campus in support of the previously announced $3.4B managed services AI cloud contract. Confirms contract is progressing to hardware deployment stage. Major balance sheet event for a company of IREN's size. Payment terms: installments within 30 days of each tranche shipping.

**MOD** — Q4 FY2026 earnings (8-K, 2026-05-26, Item 2.02). Revenue $954.4M (+47% YoY), record adjusted EBITDA $146.1M (+40% YoY). Fourth consecutive year of records. **Disclosed a landmark $4B long-term chiller agreement with a major hyperscale customer** — enormous thermal management commitment. Guided FY2027 revenue growth 20-35%, EBITDA $650-680M (+38-44% YoY). Pending spin-off of Performance Technologies segment will make MOD a purer-play DC thermal company. Gross margin under pressure (-320bps to 22.5%) from expansion costs and tariffs.

**WULF** — Acquired 1 GW "Muskie Data Campus" in Eastern Kentucky (8-K, 2026-05-26, Item 8.01). 285 acres, 500 MW initial delivery H2 2028, additional 500 MW H2 2030. 345 kV substation connected to 765 kV grid via Kentucky Power (AEP subsidiary). Company-defining expansion from ~200 MW BTC miner to GW-scale AI/HPC infrastructure. No anchor tenant announced yet — watch for customer announcement.

**ONTO** — $1.7B zero-coupon convertible notes offering (8-K, 2026-05-21, Items 1.01, 2.03). $1.5B base + $200M greenshoe exercised. 0% coupon due 2031 signals strong institutional demand for ONTO equity upside. Use of proceeds not disclosed — monitor for potential acquisition announcement.

**GOOGL** — ¥576.9B (~$3.9B USD) Japanese yen-denominated senior notes (8-K, 2026-05-21, Item 8.01). Seven tranches, maturities 2029-2066, coupons 1.965%-4.599%. Opportunistic cheap long-duration financing consistent with $75B 2026 capex budget for AI infrastructure. Confirms hyperscaler capex intent remains elevated.

**CORZ** — Former Equinix CEO Steve M. Smith appointed to board (8-K, 2026-05-26, Items 5.02, 7.01). Smith ran Equinix 2007-2018, currently CEO of Zayo. High-signal board addition for a company pivoting from BTC mining to HPC/AI hosting. Watch for new HPC contract announcements in coming 60-90 days.

**KEYS** — Record Q2 FY2026 earnings (8-K, 2026-05-19, Item 2.02). Revenue $1.72B (+31% YoY), orders >$2B, non-GAAP EPS $2.87 vs $1.70 prior year. Raised full-year guidance, Q3 guided $1.73-1.75B. CSG segment +35% YoY. Note: $100M IEEPA tariff refund inflates reported figures — strip for clean comps. Leading indicator: semiconductor, networking, and DC test equipment demand all strong.

**NNE** — NRC formally accepted Construction Permit Application for KRONOS micromodular reactor at University of Illinois (8-K, 2026-05-20, Item 8.01). Application deemed "complete" for review — years-long process ahead. Early-stage nuclear milestone; speculative but directionally positive for micro-nuclear permitting timeline.

**TLN** — Repriced and extended term loan B facilities and revolver (8-K, 2026-05-21, Item 1.01). ~$1.69B in term loans extended to 2032, SOFR spread tightened to 1.75%. Balance sheet cleanup, no new hyperscaler deal.
