# Rescore Watchlist

Post-weekly-scan tracker for stocks needing targeted rating updates. Updated after each weekly scan. Not a full quarterly rescore — only stocks where specific events shift subjective ratings.

## How to use

After each weekly scan, append to the appropriate section:
1. **Rescore Now** — thesis-shifting events already occurred; ratings are stale today
2. **Rescore After Earnings** — earnings imminent; rescore once results are in
3. **Monitor** — interesting signal but not enough to change ratings yet; revisit at quarterly rescore

Move completed rescores to the log at the bottom.
